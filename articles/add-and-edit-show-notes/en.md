# Add and Edit Show Notes

## What you'll learn

This guide shows how to add short notes to your show. Notes appear as a pinned message in chat for every viewer who joins your live. Use them for show rules, shipping announcements, discount codes, or any important callout.

## Before you start

You need:
- An approved seller account on Jamble
- A show created, or in the process of being created

## How show notes work

Show notes are short messages, up to **200 characters**, that you attach to your show. When a viewer joins your live, the note appears automatically as a **pinned message** in the chat area. The message fades on its own after a few seconds (based on estimated reading time), but viewers can re-open it at any time by tapping the **notes icon** in the top-right corner of the screen.

Notes are different from the show **description**:
- **Description**, visible on the show preview page before the show starts. Tells potential viewers what your show is about.
- **Notes**, visible only during the live show. Shared as a pinned message when viewers join.

## Add notes when creating or editing your show

When you create or edit a show, scroll to the details section. You'll see two text fields: **DESCRIPTION** and **NOTES**. The NOTES field shows a character counter in the format **(0/200)** next to its title, with the **Optional** label on the right.

![Show details form with DESCRIPTION and NOTES fields, 0/200 counter and Optional label](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/add-and-edit-show-notes__screen-1__en__v3.png)

Tap the **NOTES** field and type your message. Keep it tight: you have **200 characters** total. If you go over, the app trims the extra text automatically.

Then:
- **Creating a show**, continue the flow through to save. The notes are saved along with the other show details.
- **Editing an existing show**, save the changes with the standard save button.

## How the note appears during the live

When your show goes live and a viewer joins, the note appears as a **pinned message** over the video, with your profile picture beside the text. In the top-right corner, the **notes icon** stays visible so viewers can re-open the message at any time.

![Live show screen with pinned message displaying the note and notes icon in the top-right corner](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/add-and-edit-show-notes__screen-2__en__v3.png)

The pinned message fades automatically after a few seconds. Viewers who want to re-read it tap the **notes icon** and the message reappears.

## Edit your notes before the show starts

You can edit your notes at any time **before** the show goes live. Open your show, tap the show's edit button, update the NOTES field, and save.

**Important**, once the show starts, the edit button is no longer available on the host screen. Finalize your notes before going live. If you need to share something new during the show, say it verbally to your viewers.

## What to use notes for

Notes work best for short, important information that every viewer needs to see:

- **Show rules**, e.g. "All items start at $5. No returns on sale items"
- **Shipping promotions**, e.g. "Free shipping on orders above $20"
- **Discount codes**, e.g. "Use code LIVE30 for 30% off"
- **Lot announcements**, e.g. "Pikachu Holo PSA 9 lot starts at 9pm"
- **Product status**, e.g. "All items are used. Check photos carefully"

## Important tips

- **Keep it short.** You have 200 characters. Cut filler words and get to the point.
- **Only the notes appear during the live.** The description is only visible on the show preview page. For anything viewers need to see during the live, write it in notes.
- **The note fades on its own.** Viewers see it when they join, but the message disappears after a few seconds. For critical info, repeat it verbally during the show.
- **Finalize your notes before going live.** The show's edit button disappears once the show starts.

## Common questions

**What's the character limit for notes?**
200 characters. If you type more, the app trims at the limit.

**Can viewers re-open the note after it fades?**
Yes. The **notes icon** in the top-right corner of the show screen re-opens the pinned message.

**Do notes show up on the show preview page?**
No. Notes are only visible during the live show. The preview shows only the **description**.

**Can I edit notes after the show has started?**
Not from the show's edit button. That button is hidden on the host screen while the show is live. Update notes before going live.

**Do notes appear in replays?**
The note stays attached to the show, but the pinned-message behavior is specific to the live view. In replays, viewers may not see the auto-popup.
