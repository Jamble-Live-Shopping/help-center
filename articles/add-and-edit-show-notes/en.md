# Add and Edit Show Notes

## What you'll learn

This guide explains how to add notes to your show that are displayed to your audience during the live. Notes are a great way to share important information — show rules, shipping announcements, discount codes, or anything you want every viewer to see when they join.

## Before you start

You need:
- An approved seller account on Jamble
- A show created (or in the process of being created)

## How show notes work

Show notes are short messages (up to **200 characters**) that you attach to your show. When a viewer joins your live, the notes automatically appear as a **pinned message** in the chat area for a few seconds. Viewers can also re-read the notes at any time by tapping the notes button.

Notes are different from the show **description**:
- **Description** — visible on the show preview page before the show starts. Tells potential viewers what your show is about
- **Notes** — visible only during the live show. Shared as a pinned message when viewers join

## Step by step

### Step 1: Add notes when creating your show

When you create or edit a show, you'll see two text fields in the details section:

![Add and edit show notes form with description and notes fields](./assets/mockups/add-and-edit-show-notes__add-edit-show-notes__en.png)

Tap on the **NOTES** field and type your message. You have a maximum of **200 characters**.

### Step 2: Save your show

After typing your notes, continue with the rest of the show creation (or save your changes if editing). The notes are saved along with all other show details.

### Step 3: See how notes appear during your live

When your show is live and a viewer joins, your notes appear as a **pinned message** in the chat area:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│                                 │
│  ┌───────────────────────────┐  │
│  │ [Your avatar]             │  │
│  │ Free shipping on 3+ items!│  │
│  │ Use code: LIVE30          │  │
│  └───────────────────────────┘  │
│                                 │
│  Chat messages below...         │
│                                 │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

The pinned message shows your profile picture and note text. It auto-dismisses after a few seconds (based on reading time), but viewers can bring it back by tapping the **notes icon** in the top-right area of the show.

### Step 4: Edit notes before or during the show

You can edit your notes anytime before the show starts by editing the show. You can also edit them during the live — tap the **edit button** (pencil icon) and update your notes in the show editor.

## What to use notes for

Notes work best for short, important information that every viewer needs to see:

- **Show rules** — "All items start at R$ 5. No returns on sale items"
- **Shipping promotions** — "Free shipping on orders above R$ 100!"
- **Discount codes** — "Use code LIVE30 for 30% off"
- **Schedule info** — "Next show: Friday 8pm - Sneakers edition"
- **Product warnings** — "All items are used. Check photos carefully"

## Important tips

- **Keep it short.** You have 200 characters. Be direct — cut filler words and get to the point
- **Only the notes appear during the live.** The description is only visible on the show preview page. If you want viewers to see a message during the show, put it in notes
- **Notes auto-dismiss.** Viewers see your notes when they join, but the message fades after a few seconds. For critical info, repeat it verbally during the show
- **Update notes during the show.** If something changes (e.g., you run out of a product or change the deal), edit your notes so new joiners see the updated information

## Common questions

**What's the character limit for notes?**
200 characters. Keep it concise.

**Can viewers see notes after they dismiss?**
Yes. A **notes icon** appears in the top area of the show screen. Tapping it re-displays the pinned message.

**Do notes show up in the show preview?**
No. Notes are only visible during the live show. The show preview page shows the **description** only.

**Can I add notes after the show has started?**
Yes. You can edit your show during the live to add or update notes. The updated notes will be shown to new viewers who join after the change.

**Are notes visible in show replays?**
Notes are attached to the show but the pinned message behavior is specific to live viewing. In replays, viewers may not see the auto-popup.
