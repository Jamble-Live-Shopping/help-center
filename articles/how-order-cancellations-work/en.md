# How order cancellations work

## What you'll learn

When you can cancel an order on Jamble, what triggers an automatic cancellation, and how long it takes for the refund to land on your original payment method.

## Before you start

You need:
- An order the seller has not confirmed yet (status **Created**), OR
- An order that has passed a seller deadline (automatic cancellation)

## When you can cancel

You can cancel an order **before the seller confirms the sale**, while the status is still **Created**.

Once the seller confirms, the **Cancel Order** button disappears. In that case, ask the seller through the app chat to cancel on your behalf, or wait for the automatic deadlines described below.

## How to cancel an order

### Step 1: Open the order

Go to **Purchases** and tap the order you want to cancel. If it's still in **Created** status, you'll see the **Cancel Order** button at the top of the status card on the order detail screen.

![Order detail screen with Created status and a red Cancel Order button at the top of the status card](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/how-order-cancellations-work__order-detail-cancel-button__en__v3.png)

Tap **Cancel Order** to start the cancellation.

### Step 2: Confirm the cancellation

A sheet appears titled **Cancel this order** with a short message explaining what will happen:

![Cancel this order confirmation sheet with the message about the order being closed and two buttons Yes I cancel and No back](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/how-order-cancellations-work__cancel-order-bulletin-sheet__en__v3.png)

Tap **Yes, I cancel** to confirm, or **No, back** to exit without cancelling. There is no seller approval step: the cancellation happens immediately.

### Step 3: Track the refund

After you confirm, the order moves to **Canceled** status and the status card shows the refund timing:

![Order detail screen with Canceled status and a message stating the refund will arrive within 5 business days](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/how-order-cancellations-work__order-canceled-refund-status__en__v3.png)

The amount is refunded to the payment method used at checkout **within 5 business days**.

## Automatic cancellations

Jamble cancels an order automatically in two situations, without any action from you:

- **The seller does not confirm within 3 days.** You're refunded and the order is closed.
- **The seller confirms but does not ship within 10 days.** The order moves to a shipping-timeout state, and if the item is still not posted, you're automatically refunded.

In both cases, the refund lands on your original payment method within 5 business days after the cancellation.

## Common questions

**Can I cancel after the seller has confirmed the sale?**
Not directly. Once the status leaves Created, the Cancel Order button is no longer shown. Message the seller through the app chat to request a cancellation, or contact support if they don't respond.

**How long until I get the refund?**
Within 5 business days, on the payment method used at checkout. The clock starts when the order moves to Canceled status.

**Where does the refund go?**
Always back to the same payment method used for the original purchase. It cannot be redirected to a different card or another method.

**Will I be charged any fee?**
No. When an order is cancelled, the amount is fully refunded, including shipping.

**What if the item has already been shipped?**
Once a parcel is in transit, in-app cancellation is no longer possible. Contact support and Jamble will coordinate a return with the seller if needed.

**Does the cancellation show on the seller's history?**
Yes. Cancellations triggered by an expired seller deadline count against the seller's reputation. Cancellations the buyer initiates before confirmation are reputation-neutral.
