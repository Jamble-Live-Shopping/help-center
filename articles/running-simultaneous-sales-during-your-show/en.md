# Running Simultaneous Sales During Your Show

## What you'll learn

This guide explains how to run more than one sale at the same time during your live show on Jamble. Running simultaneous sales lets you keep the pace high and maximize your selling time.

## Before you start

You need:
- An approved seller account on Jamble
- A live show with multiple products added
- Familiarity with how sales work (see "Creating and Managing Real-time Offers")

## How simultaneous sales work

The ability to overlap sales depends on the **sell mode**:

- **Buy It Now** — you can pin and start a new product while a Buy It Now sale is still active. The previous sale continues running in the background
- **Real-time offers / Sudden Death** — you must wait for the active sale to finish before pinning a new product. These sell modes require your audience's full attention on one item at a time

This means simultaneous sales work best when combining **Buy It Now items** (which run independently) with your actively presented items.

## Step by step

### Step 1: Start a Buy It Now sale

Pin a Buy It Now product and tap **Start**. The product becomes available for purchase at the fixed price.

### Step 2: Pin your next product while Buy It Now is running

While the Buy It Now sale is active, open your product list and **Pin** a different product. Tap **Start** on this new product.

The new sale becomes the **pinned** (featured) sale that your audience sees front and center. The Buy It Now sale continues running in the background — buyers can still purchase it.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│                                 │
│  [PINNED: Product B]            │
│  Real-time offers - active      │
│                                 │
│  R$ 25,00  ⏱ 0:45  [3 offers]  │
│                                 │
│  ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─ ─  │
│  Product A (Buy It Now)         │
│  still available in background  │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Step 3: Manage overlapping sales

When you have multiple sales running:
- The **pinned sale** is the one featured on screen
- Buy It Now sales in the background remain available for purchase
- Each sale operates independently

**Important:** For real-time offers and sudden death sales, you cannot pin a new product until the current sale finishes. Wait for the timer to end, then move to your next item.

## When to use simultaneous sales

### Quick turnover with Buy It Now

If you're selling multiple Buy It Now items, you can start several at once. Buyers purchase at the fixed price without needing your attention on each individual sale.

### Overlap for pacing

When a real-time offer sale is in its final countdown, you can already pin and prepare the next product. The moment the first sale ends, you immediately start presenting the next item without dead time.

### Mix of sell modes

Run a Buy It Now item in the background (self-service for buyers) while actively presenting a real-time offer item to your audience. This maximizes your selling time.

## Important tips

- **Don't overdo it.** Running too many sales at once can confuse your audience. Start with one at a time and add overlaps gradually as you get comfortable
- **Always pin the product you're talking about.** The pinned product is what your audience sees. If you're describing an item, make sure it's the pinned one
- **Buy It Now is the best candidate for background sales.** These don't need your active promotion since buyers purchase at a fixed price
- **Real-time offer items need your attention.** Competition is driven by your energy and presentation. These should be your pinned (front and center) items
- **Watch your product requests.** If your audience is requesting a specific product while you're running another sale, consider queuing it as the next pinned item

## Common questions

**Can I run two real-time offer sales at the same time?**
No. For real-time offers and sudden death, you must wait for the active sale to finish before starting the next one. These sell modes require focused audience attention.

**Can I overlap Buy It Now with a real-time offer sale?**
Yes. This is the main use case for simultaneous sales. Start a Buy It Now item, then pin and run a real-time offer item while the Buy It Now continues in the background.

**Can buyers still purchase a non-pinned Buy It Now item?**
Yes. Buy It Now items that are running in the background remain available for purchase even if you've pinned a different product.

**What happens if two sales end at the same time?**
Each sale resolves independently. The system handles concurrent endings without issues. Winners are determined separately for each sale.

**Should I announce when I'm running multiple sales?**
Yes. Let your audience know: "I'm starting this item while the sneakers are still running — you can still place offers on those!" Clear communication prevents confusion.
