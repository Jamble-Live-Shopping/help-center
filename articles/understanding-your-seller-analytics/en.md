# Understanding Your Seller Analytics

## What you'll learn

This guide explains where to find your performance data on Jamble — your sales numbers, revenue, viewer stats, and financial information. You'll learn how to read your post-show dashboard, check your earnings, and track your progress over time.

## Before you start

You need:
- An approved seller account on Jamble
- At least one completed show (to see post-show stats)

## Where to find your analytics

Your performance data on Jamble is available in three places:

1. **Post-show dashboard** — detailed stats for each show, shown immediately after you end a live
2. **Your wallet** — financial overview with earnings, payouts, and transaction history
3. **Your profile** — high-level stats visible to you and your followers

## Post-show dashboard

After you end a show, a summary overlay appears automatically. Tap **"My Dashboard"** to see the full breakdown.

![Seller dashboard welcome screen showing username and My Dashboard button](./assets/mockups/understanding-your-seller-analytics__seller-dashboard-welcome__en.png)

### What the dashboard shows

The dashboard displays key metrics from your show:

- ****Total**** — Total revenue from all sales during the show (in R$)
- ****After Fees**** — Your actual earnings after Jamble's 14% commission
- ****Sold**** — Number of products sold
- ****Buyers**** — Number of unique buyers who purchased
You'll also see a list of all items sold during the show with individual prices, and items that went unsold.

### Using the dashboard for improvement

Pay attention to:
- **Sold vs. Unsold ratio** — if many products went unsold, consider adjusting your starting prices, improving your product descriptions, or scheduling shows at times when more buyers are online
- **Buyers count** — a high buyer count relative to your audience means your products are appealing. A low count might mean your audience is watching but not buying — try engaging more during sales
- **Revenue per item** — compare this across shows to understand which product categories perform best for you

## Your wallet

To check your financial data:

1. Go to your profile
2. Open **Settings**
3. Tap **"My Wallet"** in the SELL section

![My Wallet screen shows available balance of R$ 1,250.00 with bank details, withdraw button, and payout history](./assets/mockups/understanding-your-seller-analytics__my-wallet__en.png)

### What you'll see in your wallet

- **Available balance** — how much you can withdraw right now
- **Bank details** — your registered bank account for PIX payouts
- **Withdraw button** — request a payout to your bank account
- **Payout History** — tap the clock icon to see a list of all past payouts with dates and amounts

## Your profile stats

Your profile page shows basic stats that are visible to both you and anyone who visits your profile:

- **Followers** — number of users following you
- **Shows** — number of shows you've hosted
- **Sales** — cumulative sales data

These are high-level vanity metrics. For detailed performance data, use your post-show dashboard and wallet.

## During a live show

While you're hosting, you can track performance in real time:

- **Real-time sales display** (top-left of screen) — shows your running total of sales, revenue in R$, and average price per item. This updates live as sales close
- **Viewer count** (top-right, red badge) — shows how many people are currently watching your show. Tap it to see a list of viewers

## Viewing past shows

You can revisit your past shows to review performance:

1. Go to your profile
2. Scroll to your shows list
3. Tap any past show to see its details, including products sold and revenue

## Important tips

- **Review your dashboard after every show.** Even a quick glance at sold vs. unsold products and buyer count will help you improve over time
- **Track your average price per item.** If it's declining across shows, you might need to improve product quality or presentation
- **Use payout history to track your income.** This gives you a clear record of all money received from Jamble
- **Viewer count during shows is your live pulse.** If viewers drop during a slow moment, try engaging the chat, starting a sale, or switching to a more exciting product

## Common questions

**Is there an analytics dashboard with graphs or trends?**
Currently, Jamble does not have a standalone analytics dashboard with historical charts. Your performance data is available per-show (post-show dashboard) and financially (wallet). For trends over time, you can compare your post-show dashboards across multiple shows.

**Can I export my sales data?**
There is no export feature currently. You can view your data in the app through the post-show dashboard and wallet.

**Does Jamble show me my conversion rate?**
Not directly. But you can estimate it by comparing your viewer count (shown during the live) to your buyer count (shown in the dashboard).

**How accurate is the viewer count?**
The viewer count shows the number of people currently watching at any moment. It updates in real time. The total audience count in your dashboard shows unique viewers who joined at any point during the show.

**When does the post-show dashboard become available?**
Immediately after you end your show. It appears automatically as an overlay, and you can access it at any time after by tapping on the past show.
