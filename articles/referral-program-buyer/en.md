# Buyer Referral Program

## What you'll learn

This guide explains how Jamble's Buyer Referral Program works. You'll learn how to invite friends to shop on Jamble, how you earn $100 and your friend earns $75 in credits, and how to track your referrals in the app.

## Before you start

You need:

- An active Jamble account
- The Jamble app installed on your phone

## How the program works

Invite a friend to shop on Jamble. When they create an account and buy their first item at a Show, you earn $100 in credit and they earn a $75 starter bonus.

- **You earn $100 in credit** when your friend makes their first purchase at a Show
- **Your friend earns $75 in credit** as soon as they redeem your invite code
- **Credits can be used on any purchase** made at a Show on Jamble
- **You can refer as many friends as you want**, with no referral limit

## Step by step

### Step 1: Open the Invite Friends screen

In your profile, tap **Invite Friends**. You'll see the program steps, your $100 reward, and the **Share Friends** button.

![Invite Friends screen showing program steps, $100 credit reward, and Share Friends button](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/referral-program-buyer__buyer-overview__en__v3.png)

The screen shows the full flow: **share your code**, **your friend redeems and buys**, **you receive $100**.

### Step 2: Share your link

Tap **Share Friends** to open your phone's sharing menu. You can send it via WhatsApp, Instagram, SMS, or any other app.

The link already contains your code, so your friend doesn't need to type anything. They just need to tap the link and download the app.

**Tip**: WhatsApp is the most effective channel in Brazil. Your friend enters Jamble in just a few taps.

### Step 3: Your friend redeems and purchases

When your friend opens the app for the first time with your link:

1. **They redeem the code** and $75 appears in their account right away
2. **They have 14 days to buy 1 item at a Show** using the credit
3. **You receive $100** as soon as their purchase is confirmed

Your friend's bonus expires 14 days after sign-up. Your $100 reward expires 30 days after being credited.

## How to track your referrals

On the Invite Friends screen you can open **Ongoing Referrals** (friends who redeemed the code but haven't purchased yet) or **Past Referrals** (completed referrals).

![Past Referrals screen showing $200 earned in credits and list of friends with Completed status](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/referral-program-buyer__buyer-past-list__en__v3.png)

Each friend on the list shows their name and status:

- **Days Left** for friends still within the 14-day window
- **Completed** when your friend made their first purchase and you received the credit
- **Deadline Missed** if your friend didn't purchase within 14 days

## How credits work

Referral credits are applied automatically at checkout for your next purchase at a Show. They work as credit on your Jamble account and cover any part of the order (product and shipping).

- **Your $100 credit** expires 30 days after being credited
- **Your friend's credit ($75)** expires 14 days after sign-up
- **Credits only work on purchases made at Shows**, not in other areas of the app

## Important tips

- **Share via WhatsApp** so your friend enters Jamble in just a few taps
- **Your code is unique and permanent**, based on your username. No need to generate a new one for each invite
- **The referral only works for new accounts**. Anyone who already has Jamble can't redeem a code
- **Your friend needs to purchase at a Show**, not anywhere else in the app
- **Remind your friend** to use their credit before 14 days, or they'll lose it

## Frequently asked questions

**Where do I find my referral code?**
Go to your profile and tap **Invite Friends**. Your code appears there. You can also share directly with the **Share Friends** button, so your friend receives the link with the code already included.

**My friend signed up but I didn't receive my reward. Why?**
The reward is only credited after your friend purchases 1 item at a Show. If they redeemed the code but haven't purchased yet, the referral stays in **Ongoing Referrals**.

**How much time does my friend have to complete the referral?**
They have 14 days after creating the account to make their first purchase at a Show. After that deadline, the referral expires and neither of you receives the credit.

**Do credits work on any purchase?**
Credits are applied to any purchase made at a Show, covering both product and shipping. They don't work outside of Shows (for example, on direct purchases from a product page outside the live).

**Can I refer someone who already has a Jamble account?**
No. Codes only work for brand new accounts. If the person already has Jamble, the code won't be accepted.

**Is there a limit on how many friends I can refer?**
No limit. You can invite as many friends as you want and earn $100 for each one who makes their first purchase.

**I want to refer friends as sellers, how do I do that?**
If your friend is going to become a seller (not just a buyer), use the Seller Referral Program. The reward there is paid in cash, not credit.
