# Buyer Referral Program

## What you'll learn

This guide explains how Jamble's Buyer Referral Program works. You'll learn how to invite friends to shop on Jamble, how you earn a credit when they make their first purchase, and how to track your referrals in the app.

## Before you start

You need:

- An active Jamble account
- The Jamble app installed on your phone
- A verified phone number, required to unlock sharing

## How the program works

Invite a friend to shop on Jamble. When they create an account with your code and buy their first item at a Show, you both earn credit.

- **You earn a credit** when your friend makes their first purchase at a Show
- **Your friend earns a welcome credit** as soon as they redeem your invite code
- **Credits can be used on any purchase** made at a Show on Jamble
- **You can refer as many friends as you want**, with no referral limit

The exact credit amount is shown right on the **Invite Friends** screen and may change when Jamble adjusts the campaign. The screen in the app is always the official source.

## Open the Invite Friends screen

In your profile, tap **Invite Friends** to see the program steps and the share button.

![Invite Friends screen showing the 3 program steps, the reward, and the Share Friends and I have a referral code buttons](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/referral-program-buyer__buyer-overview__en__v3.png)

The screen shows the full flow: **share your code**, **your friend redeems and buys**, **you receive the credit**. Just below the steps, you see how much is pending in **Ongoing Referrals** and how much you have already earned in **Past Referrals**.

## Share your link

Tap **Share Friends** to open your phone's sharing menu. You can send it via WhatsApp, Instagram, SMS, or any other app.

The link already contains your code, so your friend doesn't need to type anything. They just need to tap the link and download the app.

**Tip**: WhatsApp is the most used channel in Brazil. Your friend enters Jamble in just a few taps.

## How your friend redeems the code

If your friend already has the app open, they can enter the program from the **Invite Friends** screen and tap **I have a referral code**.

![Redeem a code screen with the Enter your code field and the Redeem button](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/referral-program-buyer__redeem-code__en__v3.png)

They type the code into the **Enter your code....** field and tap **Redeem**. When the code is accepted, they see a **Code Redeemed** confirmation and the welcome credit appears in their account.

When your friend opens the app for the first time using your link, the code is applied automatically, no typing needed.

## When you receive the credit

The reward lands in your Jamble account as soon as your friend buys their first item at a Show. Before that, the referral sits in **Ongoing Referrals** with the estimated amount and the time remaining.

## Track your referrals

On the **Invite Friends** screen, open **Ongoing Referrals** to see friends who redeemed the code but haven't purchased yet, or **Past Referrals** to see completed referrals.

![Past Referrals screen showing the total earned in credits and a list of friends with Completed and Deadline Missed status](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/referral-program-buyer__buyer-past-list__en__v3.png)

Each friend on the list shows their name and status:

- **days Left** for friends still within the window
- **Completed** when your friend made their first purchase and you received the credit
- **Deadline Missed** if your friend didn't purchase within the window

## How credits work

Referral credits are applied automatically at checkout for your next purchase at a Show. They work as a balance on your Jamble account and cover any part of the order, including product and shipping.

- **Credits only work on purchases made at Shows**, not in other areas of the app
- **Each credit has an expiry date**, shown on your wallet screen
- **Credits are not converted into cash** and cannot be withdrawn

## Important tips

- **Share via WhatsApp** so your friend enters Jamble in just a few taps
- **Your code is unique and permanent**, based on your username. No need to generate a new one for each invite
- **The referral only works for new accounts**. Anyone who already has Jamble can't redeem a code
- **Your friend needs to purchase at a Show**, not anywhere else in the app
- **Remind your friend** to use their credit before it expires, or they will lose it

## Privacy

When your friend signs up with your code, the seller does not see that the purchase came from a referral. The referral stays between you, your friend, and Jamble.

## Frequently asked questions

**Where do I find my referral code?**
Go to your profile and tap **Invite Friends**. Your code appears there. You can also share directly with the **Share Friends** button, so your friend receives the link with the code already included.

**My friend signed up but I didn't receive the credit. Why?**
The credit is only released after your friend purchases 1 item at a Show. If they redeemed the code but haven't purchased yet, the referral stays in **Ongoing Referrals**.

**How much time does my friend have to complete the referral?**
The deadline shows on the **Ongoing Referrals** screen as **days Left** next to their name. After that deadline, the referral expires and neither of you receives the credit.

**Do credits work on any purchase?**
Credits are applied to any purchase made at a Show, covering both product and shipping. They don't work outside of Shows.

**Can I refer someone who already has a Jamble account?**
No. Codes only work for brand new accounts. If the person already has Jamble, the code won't be accepted.

**Is there a limit on how many friends I can refer?**
There is no limit. You can invite as many friends as you want and earn a credit for each one who makes their first purchase.

**I want to refer friends as sellers, how do I do that?**
If your friend is going to become a seller, not just a buyer, use the Seller Referral Program. The reward there is paid in cash, not credit.
