# Set Up and Start a Payout

## What you'll learn

How to register your bank details on Jamble and withdraw your earnings to your bank account.

## Before you start

You need:
- An approved seller account on Jamble
- A Brazilian bank account in your name
- Your CPF (or CNPJ, if you sell as a company)
- Available earnings in your wallet (at least one completed sale)

## Step 1: Open your wallet

On your profile, tap **Settings**. In the **SELL** section, tap **My Wallet**.

![Settings screen showing the SELL section with My Sales, My Wallet, and Shipping Preferences](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/set-up-and-start-a-payout__settings-menu__en.png)

## Step 2: Register your bank (first time only)

On your first visit, the wallet shows **R$ 0.00** in **Available to Withdraw** and the **Register Bank** button in place of the withdrawal button. Tap it to get started.

![My Wallet screen on first visit, showing Available to Withdraw R$ 0.00 and Register Bank button](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/set-up-and-start-a-payout__wallet-register-bank__en.png)

A **Registration** alert appears with three options:

![Registration alert explaining identity verification via Pagar.me, with three buttons: Individual, Company, Later](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/set-up-and-start-a-payout__registration-type-picker__en.png)

Choose the option that matches your situation:
- **Individual** if you sell as a private person (use your CPF)
- **Company** if you sell as a registered company (use your CNPJ)
- **Later** to skip registration for now

Registration opens an external form provided by **Pagar.me**, our payment partner, to verify your identity and bank details. Follow the form steps to completion.

## Step 3: Check your bank details

After registration, go back to **My Wallet**. The **Bank Details** section shows your registered account. The **Update** button lets you change the account anytime.

![My Wallet screen after registration: Available to Withdraw R$ 350.00 with Withdraw button, Bank Details card (Banco do Brasil, branch, account) with Update button, and Pending line R$ 120.00](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/set-up-and-start-a-payout__wallet-with-bank__en.png)

Check that the name, bank, branch, and account are correct. Wrong details will cause the withdrawal to fail.

## Step 4: Withdraw your earnings

With earnings in **Available to Withdraw**, the **Withdraw** button becomes active. Tap it to send your entire available balance via PIX to your registered account.

It's just one tap. A confirmation appears, you confirm, and that's it. The transfer usually arrives at your bank in 1 to 2 business days via PIX.

**Note:** you can withdraw once every 24 hours. After a withdrawal, the button shows a countdown until the next withdrawal is available.

## Step 5: Track your payout

In the top right of the **My Wallet** screen, tap the **clock icon** to open **Payouts History**. There you see each withdrawal, the amount, the date, and the current status.

![Payouts History screen listing three withdrawals with ID, amount in Real, status (Completed, Processing, Failed), and date](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/set-up-and-start-a-payout__payouts-history__en.png)

## Payout statuses

- **Created** the request was received
- **Processing** Pagar.me is sending the transfer to your bank
- **Pending** the transfer is awaiting processing
- **Completed** the money arrived in your account
- **Failed** something went wrong (see the resolution article)
- **Canceled** the withdrawal was canceled

## Important tips

- **Register your bank BEFORE your first sale.** Don't wait until you have earnings. With your bank registered from the start, withdrawals are available whenever you need them
- **Withdrawing is just one tap.** Tap **Withdraw** and your entire available balance goes to your bank
- **Transfers arrive quickly.** Usually 1 to 2 business days. Track it in **Payouts History**
- **One withdrawal per day.** You can withdraw once every 24 hours, so plan accordingly
- **Keep your bank details up to date.** If you change banks, update before withdrawing. Wrong details will cause the withdrawal to fail
- **Individual or Company.** Choose the correct type during registration. MEIs, LTDAs, and other registered companies: **Company** with CNPJ. Otherwise, **Individual** with CPF

## Frequently asked questions

**How do I receive my money?**
Via PIX. When you tap **Withdraw**, Jamble sends your available balance to your registered account.

**How long does a withdrawal take?**
Usually 1 to 2 business days to arrive at your bank.

**Is there a minimum amount?**
There is no fixed minimum amount displayed on the button. If the balance in **Available to Withdraw** is very low, the button may be inactive. Accumulate more sales and try again.

**How often can I withdraw?**
Once every 24 hours.

**Can I withdraw to any bank?**
To any Brazilian bank account that accepts PIX and is in your name (matching your registered CPF or CNPJ).

**I don't see the Withdraw button. Why?**
Either you don't have available earnings yet, or your bank isn't registered. If the button says **Register Bank**, complete the registration first.

**Can I change my bank account?**
Yes. In the **Bank Details** section of your wallet, tap **Update** to change the account.

**What if my withdrawal fails?**
Check that your bank details are correct. Wrong details are the most common cause. Correct them in **Update** and try again. If the problem persists, contact support.
