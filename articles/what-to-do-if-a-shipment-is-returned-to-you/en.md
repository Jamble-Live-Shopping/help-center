# What to Do If a Shipment Is Returned to You

## What you'll learn

This guide explains what to do when a package you shipped is returned to you by Correios. You'll learn the common reasons, how to handle the situation, and how to prevent it from happening again.

## Before you start

You need:
- An approved seller account on Jamble
- A package that was returned to your address by Correios

## Why packages get returned

Correios may return a package to the sender for several reasons:

| Reason | What happened |
|--------|--------------|
| **Wrong address** | The address on the label doesn't exist or is incomplete (missing number, wrong CEP, etc.) |
| **Recipient not found** | Correios attempted delivery but nobody was available to receive, and the pickup deadline passed |
| **Package refused** | The buyer refused to accept the delivery |
| **Unclaimed at agency** | The buyer was notified to pick up the package at a Correios agency but didn't collect it within the deadline |
| **Address inaccessible** | The address is in an area Correios can't deliver to (restricted access, unsafe area, etc.) |

## Step by step

### Step 1: Check your tracking

When a package is returned, the tracking status will show something like "Returned to sender" or "Object returned." Check the tracking to understand why it was returned.

### Step 2: Contact the buyer

Reach out to the buyer through the app chat:
- Let them know the package was returned
- Ask if there was an address issue
- Find out if they still want the item

### Step 3: Decide the next step

Based on your conversation with the buyer:

**If the buyer still wants the item:**
- Get the correct address from the buyer
- Contact support to arrange a new shipment. A new label may need to be generated with the corrected address
- Reship the package once you have the new label

**If the buyer wants a refund:**
- Contact support to initiate a cancellation and refund
- The buyer will be refunded, and you keep your item

**If you can't reach the buyer:**
- Contact support. They can attempt to reach the buyer and help resolve the situation

### Step 4: Contact support

In most return situations, you'll need support's help to:
- Issue a new shipping label with a corrected address
- Process a refund if the buyer no longer wants the item
- Handle any disputes about who's responsible for the return

## Who pays for the return shipping?

When Correios returns a package, the return shipping cost depends on the situation:

- **If the address was wrong due to buyer error** — the buyer is generally responsible
- **If the package was refused by the buyer** — contact support to determine the next steps
- **If the address was wrong due to a label error** — contact support for assistance

Support handles return shipping costs on a case-by-case basis.

## How to prevent returned shipments

Most returns are preventable. Here are the most effective steps:

1. **Generate labels promptly.** The sooner you ship, the more likely the buyer is still at the address and expecting the package
2. **Double-check your own address.** If your sender address is wrong, the package may be "returned" to a wrong address — and you lose it
3. **Encourage buyers to use complete addresses.** Apartment number, building name, and reference points help Correios find the recipient
4. **Ship during business hours.** Dropping off packages during Correios operating hours ensures they enter the system the same day
5. **Package items well.** Damaged packages may be returned by Correios if they can't safely continue transit

## Important tips

- **Don't ignore a returned package.** Act quickly — the buyer is waiting. Contact them and support as soon as the package arrives back
- **Keep the original packaging.** If you need to reship, having the item already packaged saves time
- **Document everything.** Take photos of the returned package, especially if it arrived damaged. This helps with insurance claims
- **Check for damage.** If the item was damaged during the return transit, note it before making any decisions about reshipping

## Common questions

**Will I be penalized if a package is returned?**
Not automatically. Returns due to buyer address errors are not held against you. However, if returns happen frequently due to late shipping on your part, it may affect your seller reputation.

**Can I reship the package on my own?**
You'll need a new shipping label from Jamble (or you can generate your own and add the tracking number). Don't reuse the old label — it's already been processed in the system.

**How long does it take for a returned package to come back to me?**
Return shipping with Correios typically takes as long as the original delivery route. It could be a few days to a couple of weeks depending on the distance.

**What if the buyer's address was correct but Correios still returned it?**
This can happen if nobody was home to receive the package and the buyer didn't pick it up from the Correios agency within the deadline. Contact support — they can help coordinate a reshipment.

**What if the returned package is damaged?**
Document the damage with photos. If the package was insured, contact support to file an insurance claim. If not, you and support will work together to find a fair resolution with the buyer.

**Can I refuse to reship?**
If the return was caused by the buyer (wrong address, refusal), you can work with support to arrange a refund instead. You're not obligated to reship if the buyer doesn't cooperate.
