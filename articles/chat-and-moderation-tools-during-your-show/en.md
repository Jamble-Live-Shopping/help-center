# Chat and Moderation Tools During Your Show

## What you'll learn

This guide explains how to manage your show's chat, deal with problematic users, and set up moderators to keep your live running smoothly. You'll learn every moderation action available to you as a seller.

## Before you start

You need:
- An approved seller account on Jamble
- A scheduled or live show
- Optional, someone you trust and follow to make a moderator

## How chat moderation works on Jamble

Jamble does not use typed commands (like /ban or /mute). All actions are done by tapping on a message or a username in the chat. A menu appears with the options available to you, based on your role.

There are three roles with moderation powers:

- **Seller (host)**, deletes messages, blocks users from the show
- **Moderator**, deletes messages, blocks users from the show
- **Admin (Jamble staff)**, all of the above, plus mute and ban accounts

As a seller, you have full moderation control over your own show.

## Opening the actions menu

To moderate any message, tap on the message or on the username in the chat. A menu slides up from the bottom with the available actions.

![iOS chat actions menu with options Reply, See profile, Report, Block from show, Delete this message, Cancel](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/chat-and-moderation-tools-during-your-show__chat-action-sheet__en__v2.png)

The destructive actions (in red) are **Block from show** and **Delete this message**. The neutral actions (in purple or blue) are **Reply**, **See profile** and **Report**.

Tap the action you want to run, or **Cancel** to close the menu without changing anything.

## Deleting a message

If someone posts something inappropriate in your chat, open the menu on the message and tap **Delete this message**. The message disappears from the chat for every viewer instantly.

You can delete any message in your show. Moderators can too, except messages posted by you, by other moderators, or by Jamble staff.

## Blocking a user from your show

If someone is being disruptive, open the menu on the username and tap **Block from show**. A confirmation dialog appears before the block.

![iOS Block from show confirmation dialog with message The user will not able to join this show and Cancel and Block from show buttons](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/chat-and-moderation-tools-during-your-show__block-from-show-confirm__en__v2.png)

Tap **Block from show** in red to confirm. The user is removed from the show immediately and cannot rejoin. Their messages stay visible, but they no longer participate.

This is a **show-level block**, it only applies to the current show. They can still join your future shows unless you block them at the account level.

## Blocking a user from your account

For a permanent block across all your shows, open the menu on the username and tap **Block**. This blocks the user at the account level. They will no longer be able to interact with you on the platform.

To see and manage your blocked users, go to **Settings > Blocked profiles**.

## Unblocking a user

To unblock someone from your show, tap on their username and select the show unblock option. For an account-level unblock, go to **Settings > Blocked profiles** and remove them from the list.

## Reporting a user

If someone violates Jamble's community guidelines, open the menu on the username and tap **Report**. You pick a reason, **False information** or **Inappropriate content**, and the report is sent to the Jamble team.

Reports are anonymous, the user does not know you reported them.

## Setting up moderators

Moderators are trusted users who help manage the chat during your show. They can delete messages and block disruptive users, so you can stay focused on selling.

### How to add a moderator

1. Go to **Settings** in the app
2. Tap **My Moderators**
3. You see the list of profiles you follow
4. Tap the **Add** button next to the person you want to make a moderator

![Moderators screen listing three profiles with avatars, display name, handle, and Add buttons in purple and Remove buttons in gray](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/chat-and-moderation-tools-during-your-show__my-moderators__en__v2.png)

The **Add** button in purple confirms the promotion. The **Remove** button in gray takes someone off the list. You can only add people you follow.

### How to remove a moderator

Go to **Settings > My Moderators** and tap **Remove** next to the person you want to take off the list.

### What moderators see

When a moderator joins one of your shows, they see a notification: *You are one of [your name]'s moderators! You can remove sensitive messages and block users from the Show in extreme cases.*

Moderators see extra options when they tap on messages in your chat:

- **Delete this message**, removes the message for everyone
- **Block from show**, removes the user from the show

Moderators cannot delete messages from you (the seller), other moderators, or Jamble staff.

## Show notes (pinned message)

You can add **notes** to your show that appear to your audience. Notes are set when creating or editing a show, up to 200 characters.

Use notes for:

- Show rules ("No returns on sale items")
- Special offers ("Free shipping above $20")
- Announcements ("New drops every Thursday")

Notes are visible to all viewers throughout the show.

## Important tips

- **Set up moderators before your show**, don't wait until you need one. Have a trusted friend ready before going live
- **Don't over-moderate**, block disruptive users but don't silence criticism. Buyers who feel heard are more likely to buy
- **Use Block from show for one-off issues**, if someone is just being annoying in one show, a show-level block is enough. Save account-level blocks for serious cases
- **Address trolls quickly and move on**, don't engage, just block and continue. Your audience will appreciate a smooth experience
- **Use notes wisely**, a pinned note with your show rules or shipping info saves you from answering the same questions many times

## Common questions

**Can I use typed commands like /ban or /mute?**
No. Jamble uses a tap-based menu instead of typed commands. Tap on any message or username to see the available actions.

**How many moderators can I have?**
There is no strict limit, but you can only add people you follow. Most sellers have 1 to 3 trusted moderators.

**Can moderators sell products during my show?**
No. Moderators only manage chat. They cannot start sales, pin products, or access host tools.

**Can a blocked user see my future shows?**
If you used Block from show, the block only applies to the current show and they can join future shows. If you used Block at the account level, they are blocked from all your content.

**Do moderators get any special badge?**
Moderators see a notification when they join your show confirming their role. The moderation tools appear when they tap on messages.
