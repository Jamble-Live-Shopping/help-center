# Chat and Moderation Tools During Your Show

## What you'll learn

This guide explains how to manage your show's chat, deal with problematic users, and use moderators to help keep your live running smoothly. You'll learn every moderation action available to you as a seller.

## Before you start

You need:
- An approved seller account on Jamble
- A scheduled or live show

## How chat moderation works on Jamble

Jamble does not use typed commands (like /ban or /mute). All moderation actions are done by **tapping on a message or a user** — a menu appears with the actions available to you based on your role.

There are three roles with moderation powers:

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Seller (host)** | Delete messages, block users from the show |
| **Moderator** | Delete messages, block users from the show |
| **Admin (Jamble staff)** | All of the above + mute, ban |

As a seller, you have full moderation control over your own show.

## Managing your chat

### Deleting a message

If someone posts something inappropriate in your chat:

1. **Tap on the message** you want to remove
2. A menu appears with options
3. Tap **"Delete this message"**

The message is removed from the chat for everyone.

You can delete any message in your show. Moderators can also delete messages, except those posted by you, other moderators, or Jamble staff.

### Blocking a user from your show

If someone is being disruptive:

1. **Tap on their message** or **tap on their username** in the chat
2. A menu appears
3. Tap **"Block from show"**

The user is immediately removed from your show and cannot rejoin. Their messages remain visible but they can no longer participate.

This is a **show-level block** — it only applies to the current show. They can still watch your future shows unless you block them at the account level.

### Blocking a user from your account

For a permanent block across all your shows:

1. **Tap on their message** or **tap on their username**
2. Tap **"Block"**

This blocks the user at the account level. They won't be able to interact with you across the platform.

To see and manage your blocked users, go to **Settings > Blocked profiles**.

### Unblocking a user

To unblock someone from your show, tap on their username and select **"Unblock from show."** For account-level unblocks, go to **Settings > Blocked profiles** and remove them from the list.

### Reporting a user

If someone violates Jamble's community guidelines:

1. **Tap on their message** or **tap on their username**
2. Tap **"Report"**

This sends a report to the Jamble team for review. Reports are anonymous — the user won't know you reported them.

## Setting up moderators

Moderators are trusted users who can help you manage your chat during a show. They can delete messages and block disruptive users, so you can focus on selling.

### How to add a moderator

1. Go to **Settings** in the app
2. Tap **"My Moderators"**
3. You'll see a list of profiles you follow
4. Tap the **Add** button next to the person you want to make a moderator

```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│  My Moderators                  │
│                                 │
│  ┌───┐ username1        [Add]   │
│  │ 👤│ @user1                   │
│  └───┘                          │
│  ┌───┐ username2      [Remove]  │
│  │ 👤│ @user2 (moderator)       │
│  └───┘                          │
│                                 │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

You can only add people you follow as moderators.

### How to remove a moderator

Go to **Settings > My Moderators** and tap **"Remove"** next to the person you want to remove.

### What moderators see

When a moderator joins one of your shows, they see a notification: *"You are one of [your name]'s moderators! You can remove sensitive messages and block users from the Show in extreme cases."*

Moderators see extra options when they tap on messages in your chat:
- **Delete this message** — removes the message for everyone
- **Block from show** — removes the user from the show

Moderators cannot delete messages from you (the seller), other moderators, or Jamble staff.

## All moderation actions at a glance

| Action | What it does | Who can do it |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| **Delete this message** | Removes a chat message for everyone | Seller, Moderators |
| **Block from show** | Removes user from this show, can't rejoin | Seller, Moderators |
| **Unblock from show** | Lets a blocked user back into the show | Seller, Moderators |
| **Block** | Account-level block across all interactions | Anyone |
| **Unblock** | Removes an account-level block | Anyone |
| **Report** | Sends a report to Jamble for review | Anyone |
| **See profile** | Opens the user's profile | Anyone |

## Show notes (pinned message)

You can add **notes** to your show that appear to your audience. Notes are set when creating or editing a show — up to 200 characters.

Use notes for:
- Show rules ("No returns on sale items")
- Special offers ("Frete grátis acima de R$100")
- Announcements ("New drops every Thursday!")

Notes are visible to all viewers throughout the show.

## Important tips

- **Set up moderators before your show.** Don't wait until you need one — have a trusted friend ready as a moderator before going live
- **Don't over-moderate.** Block disruptive users but don't silence criticism. Buyers who feel heard are more likely to buy
- **Use "Block from show" for minor issues.** If someone is just being annoying in one show, a show-level block is enough. Save account-level blocks for serious cases
- **Address trolls quickly and move on.** Don't engage with trolls — just block and continue your show. Your audience will appreciate a smooth experience
- **Use notes effectively.** A pinned note with your show rules or shipping info saves you from answering the same questions repeatedly

## Common questions

**Can I use typed commands like /ban or /mute?**
No. Jamble uses a tap-based menu system instead of typed commands. Tap on any message or username to see available moderation actions.

**How many moderators can I have?**
There is no strict limit, but you can only add people you follow. Most sellers have 1-3 trusted moderators.

**Can moderators sell products during my show?**
No. Moderators can only manage chat. They cannot start sales, pin products, or access any host tools.

**Can a blocked user see my future shows?**
If you used "Block from show," the block only applies to the current show — they can join future shows. If you used "Block" (account-level), they are blocked from all your content.

**Can I see who my moderators blocked during a show?**
Blocked users are visible in the show's moderation log. You can unblock them by tapping on their name and selecting "Unblock from show."

**Do moderators get any special badge?**
Moderators see a notification when they join your show confirming their role. The moderation tools appear when they tap on messages.
