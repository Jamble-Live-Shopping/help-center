# Invite a co-host (Live Duo)

> Currently available on iPhone only.

## What you'll learn

How to invite someone to join your live show as a co-host (Jamble calls them a "guest") using **Live Duo**, what the guest can do during the show, and how to remove them when you want to end the duo.

## Before you start

- An approved seller account on Jamble (iPhone)
- You must already be live. The invite button only appears once your show is broadcasting
- At least one viewer in your show, or someone you can find through search

## How Live Duo works

Live Duo lets you bring another person on camera during your show. The guest appears in the top-left corner of your screen as a small window, while your camera stays full-screen as the main feed. Your audience sees both of you at the same time.

You can have **one guest at a time**. To switch, remove the current guest before inviting someone else.

## Step 1, open the invite sheet

While live, tap the camera-with-plus button in the top-right of your screen. The button is hidden whenever a guest is already on. If you don't see it, remove the current guest first.

A bottom sheet appears titled **Invite for a Duo**, showing the viewers currently watching your show. You can scroll the list or use the search bar to find someone by username.

![Invite for a Duo bottom sheet with search bar, grid of viewer avatars, and purple Invite your friend button at the bottom](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/invite-a-co-host-live-duo__invite-bottom-sheet__en__v3.png)

Tap a viewer to select them. The **Invite your friend** button only enables once a viewer is selected. Tap it to send the invite.

## Step 2, wait for the guest to accept

The person you invited sees a modal on their screen titled **Live Duo** with the line **You have been invited to join (your username) as a guest!**.

![Live Duo guest modal: video icon, invite line, and two buttons, Accept and Join in solid purple plus Don't Join in red text](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/invite-a-co-host-live-duo__guest-invite-modal__en__v3.png)

The guest can tap **Accept and Join** to come on. If they have not granted camera and microphone permission yet, the button reads **Allow Camera Access and Join** and the app prompts for access first. To decline, the guest taps **Don't Join**.

## Step 3, go live together

Once the guest accepts, their camera turns on and their video appears as a small window in the top-left corner of your screen. Your camera stays as the full-screen main feed and your audience sees both of you.

## Removing a guest

To end a guest's participation during the show:

1. Tap the guest's circular avatar in the top-right of your screen (the guest button)
2. An iOS alert appears titled **Remove (username) From Live Duo?**
3. Tap **Remove** (in red) to confirm, or **Cancel** to dismiss

![Native iOS alert Remove username From Live Duo? with two stacked buttons, Cancel on top and Remove in red below](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/invite-a-co-host-live-duo__remove-guest-alert__en__v3.png)

The guest is notified and goes back to being a regular viewer.

The guest can also leave on their own. Tapping their own broadcasting button shows the alert **Stop broadcasting?**, which they confirm with **Stop**.

## What the guest can and can't do

- **Can**, stream their camera and audio, be seen and heard by the audience, interact with chat, stop broadcasting and return to viewing
- **Can't**, sell products, open the product manager, end the show, invite other guests

A guest is essentially a viewer who appears on camera. Host controls stay with you.

## Important tips

- **Any viewer can be invited**, they don't need to be a seller or a follower
- **Only one guest at a time**. To switch, remove the current one first
- **Coordinate beforehand**, let your guest know they'll need to grant camera and microphone access on their phone
- **Keep it natural**, duo shows work best when both people have something to contribute
- **No invite button?** Make sure you are already live and that no guest is active

## Common questions

**Does the guest need a seller account?**
No. Any Jamble user can be a guest. They only need to grant camera and microphone permission on their iPhone.

**Can I have more than one guest?**
No. Live Duo supports one guest at a time.

**What if the guest loses connection?**
Their video window disappears from your screen. When they reconnect to the show, you can invite them again.

**Is Live Duo available on Android?**
Not yet. The feature is iPhone-only at the moment.

**Can the guest sell products during the show?**
No. Only the host (you) manages products and sales.
