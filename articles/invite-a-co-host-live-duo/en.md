# Invite a Co-Host (Live Duo)

> This feature is currently available on **iPhone only**.

## What you'll learn

This guide explains how to invite someone to join your live show as a co-host using the **Live Duo** feature. You'll learn how to invite a viewer, what your co-host can do, and how to manage the duo during your show.

## Before you start

You need:
- An approved seller account on Jamble (iPhone)
- A show that is currently live (you must already be broadcasting)
- At least one viewer in your show, or the username of someone you want to invite

## How Live Duo works

Live Duo lets you bring another person on camera during your show. Your co-host (called a "guest") appears as a small video overlay on your screen, so both of you are visible to your audience at the same time.

This is great for:
- Doing a show with a friend or business partner
- Bringing an expert to talk about a specific product
- Creating more engaging content with two personalities

You can have **one guest at a time**.

## Step by step

### Step 1: Start your show

Go live first. The Live Duo option is only available during a live show — you cannot invite a co-host before starting.

### Step 2: Tap the camera icon

While live, look for the **camera-add icon** in the top-right area of your host screen. Tap it to open the invite screen.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│  [📷+]              [viewers]   │
│                                 │
│   Your live camera feed         │
│                                 │
│                                 │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

This icon only appears when you don't already have a guest on screen.

### Step 3: Choose who to invite

A bottom sheet titled **"Invite for a Duo"** appears, showing the viewers currently watching your show. You can scroll through the list or use the **search bar** to find someone by username.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│  Invite for a Duo               │
│                                 │
│  [Search by username...]        │
│                                 │
│  ┌───┐ ┌───┐ ┌───┐             │
│  │ 👤│ │ 👤│ │ 👤│             │
│  └───┘ └───┘ └───┘             │
│  ┌───┐ ┌───┐ ┌───┐             │
│  │ 👤│ │ 👤│ │ 👤│             │
│  └───┘ └───┘ └───┘             │
│                                 │
│  ┌───────────────────────────┐  │
│  │    Invite your friend     │  │
│  └───────────────────────────┘  │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

Tap on a viewer to select them, then tap **"Invite your friend"** to send the invitation.

If nobody is watching yet, you'll see a message: *"No one in your Show? You can still invite someone outside!"*

### Step 4: Wait for them to accept

Your guest receives a notification inviting them to join. On their screen, they see:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│  Live Duo                       │
│                                 │
│  You have been invited to join  │
│  [your username] as a guest!    │
│                                 │
│  ┌───────────────────────────┐  │
│  │    Accept and Join        │  │
│  └───────────────────────────┘  │
│                                 │
│  Don't Join                     │
│                                 │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

They can tap **"Accept and Join"** (which requires camera and microphone access) or **"Don't Join"** to decline.

The invitation expires after **5 minutes**. If they don't respond in time, the invitation is automatically canceled.

### Step 5: Go live together

Once your guest accepts, their camera activates and they appear as a **small video overlay** on your screen. Your audience sees both of you.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│  ┌────────┐                     │
│  │ Guest  │        Your main    │
│  │ video  │        camera feed  │
│  └────────┘                     │
│                                 │
│                                 │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

The guest's video appears in the top-left corner as a small picture-in-picture window. Your camera stays as the main full-screen feed.

## Removing a guest

To remove your guest during the show:

1. Tap on your guest's profile icon (small circular photo in the top-right)
2. A confirmation message appears: **"Remove [username] From Live Duo?"**
3. Tap **"Remove"** to confirm

Your guest will be notified and returned to the regular audience.

The guest can also leave on their own by tapping their broadcasting button and confirming **"Stop broadcasting?"**.

## What the guest can and can't do

| Guest CAN do | Guest CANNOT do |
|--------------|-----------------|
| Stream their camera and audio | Start or end the show |
| Be seen and heard by the audience | Manage products or sales |
| Interact with the chat | Access any host tools |
| Stop broadcasting and rejoin | Invite other guests |

The guest is essentially a viewer who appears on camera — they have no host controls.

## Important tips

- **Any viewer can be invited** — they don't need to be a seller or follow you. Anyone in your audience can become a guest
- **Only one guest at a time.** If you want to switch guests, remove the current one first, then invite a new person
- **Test audio before inviting.** Make sure your guest knows they'll need camera and microphone permissions before accepting
- **Keep it natural.** Duo shows work best when both people have something to contribute — a friend who knows the products, a partner who handles questions, etc.
- **The invitation expires in 5 minutes.** If your guest misses it, you can send a new invitation

## Common questions

**Can I invite someone who isn't watching my show?**
The invite screen shows your current viewers. If nobody is watching, you'll see a message suggesting you can still invite someone — they'll need to be found via search.

**Does the guest need to be a seller?**
No. Any Jamble user can be invited as a guest. They just need camera and microphone permissions.

**Can I have more than one guest?**
No. Live Duo supports one guest at a time.

**What happens if the guest loses connection?**
Their video will disappear from your screen. They can rejoin the duo if they reconnect to the show.

**Is Live Duo available on Android?**
Not yet. Live Duo is currently available on iPhone only.

**Can the guest sell products during the show?**
No. Only the host can manage products and sales. The guest is on camera but has no host controls.
