# Direct Messages for Sellers

## What you'll learn

Where to find buyer messages in the app, what each Activity tab filter shows, and how to start a conversation from a buyer profile.

## Before you start

You need an approved seller account on Jamble and the app installed on your phone.

## Where to find them

All buyer messages live in the **Activity** tab in the app's bottom menu. It is the screen where Jamble groups everything that needs your attention: new messages, sales, purchases, alerts.

![Activity screen with the 6 filter sub-tabs (All / Messages / Unread / Purchases / Sales / Archived) and the conversation list below](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/direct-messages-for-sellers__activity-tabs__en__v3.png)

## What each filter shows

The Activity tab has 6 filters at the top:

**All** : everything that entered your Activity tab, no filter.

**Messages** : private conversations (direct messages) only.

**Unread** : conversations with messages you have not opened yet.

**Purchases** : conversations tied to items you bought from other sellers.

**Sales** : conversations tied to items you sold. **As a seller, this is the tab you open first.**

**Archived** : conversations you archived.

## How to start a message

To message someone who has not written to you yet, open that person's profile and tap **Message** next to **Follow**.

![Buyer profile with the Follow and Message buttons visible under the username](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/direct-messages-for-sellers__profile-message-cta__en__v3.png)

Your first message creates the conversation. If you have already exchanged messages before, the app opens the existing conversation instead of creating a new one.

## Notifications

Every new message appears as a push notification, as long as the **Activity** category is enabled in your notification settings. See the **Notification settings** article for details.

## Important tips

- **Start with the Sales tab.** That is where the conversations from buyers who already bought from you live (shipping, post-sale question, return).
- **Check the Unread tab daily.** Quick replies from sellers move the needle on rating and on the odds of a future sale.
- **Open the Messages tab second.** That is where the private messages that have not turned into sales live yet (pre-purchase question, lead).
- **Keep the conversation inside Jamble.** Do not share phone numbers, emails, or social handles in DMs.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can buyers message me before they buy?**
Yes. Anyone can tap **Message** on your profile to start a conversation, even if they have not bought from you yet.

**Does every sale create a conversation?**
Yes. Every completed sale has an associated conversation you can reach from the Sales tab.

**How do I know I have new messages?**
The Activity tab shows a badge in the bottom menu, and the **Unread** filter isolates the conversations with messages you have not opened yet.

**What happens if I disable the Activity notification category?**
You still receive the messages, but you only find them when you open the app. Push notifications only fire if the **Activity** category is on (see the Notification settings article).
