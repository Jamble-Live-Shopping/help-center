# Direct Messages for Sellers

## What you'll learn

This guide explains how direct messages (DMs) work for sellers on Jamble. You'll learn where to find your conversations, how to talk to buyers about orders, send photos, and manage your inbox. DMs are your main channel for communicating with buyers outside live shows.

## Before you start

You need:

- An active seller account on Jamble
- The Jamble app installed on your phone

## Where to find your messages

All your conversations live in the **Activity** tab, one of the main tabs at the bottom of the screen.

![Activity screen with All, Unread, Purchases, Sales, Archived filters and conversation list below](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/direct-messages-for-sellers__activity-tabs__en__v3.png)

At the top of the screen, filters help you organize your conversations:

- **All** shows every conversation you have
- **Unread** shows only conversations with new messages
- **Purchases** shows conversations about items you bought
- **Sales** shows conversations about items you sold, your main tab as a seller
- **Archived** shows archived conversations

As a seller, the **Sales** tab is where you spend most of your time. It shows every conversation tied to your sales: buyer questions, order confirmations, shipping discussions.

## Types of conversations

There are three conversation types on Jamble.

### Direct message

When someone taps **Message** on your profile, a simple one-to-one conversation is created between you and that person, with no product or order attached.

### Product conversation

When a buyer makes an offer on one of your products, a conversation is automatically created with the **product details at the top**. You can discuss the product, negotiate, and manage offers within this conversation.

![Product conversation with product card on top (image, name, price) and message exchange between buyer and seller below](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/direct-messages-for-sellers__dm-product-card__en__v3.png)

### Transaction conversation

When a sale is completed, a conversation is created (or updated) with the **transaction details**: product info, order status, and shipping address. This is where you coordinate shipping and answer post-sale questions. The conversation includes action buttons to confirm orders, review details, and manage the transaction.

## How to start a conversation

You can message any user on Jamble:

1. Open the person's **profile**
2. Tap **Message** next to the Follow button
3. Type your message and send

![User profile header with username, follower count, and Follow and Message buttons](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/direct-messages-for-sellers__user-profile-header__en__v3.png)

If you've already chatted with this person, the app opens the existing conversation. Otherwise, a new conversation is created when you send your first message.

Jamble also creates conversations automatically when a buyer makes an offer on your product, completes a purchase from you, or contacts you through your profile.

## What you can send

### Text messages

Type a message up to **500 characters** and tap the send button. Messages are delivered in real time.

### Photos

Tap the **camera icon** next to the text field to send photos:

- **On iOS** you select up to 6 photos from your gallery
- **On Android** you select up to 10 photos from your gallery, or take a photo directly with the camera

Photos are useful to show product details, packaging, shipping labels, or to answer buyer questions about condition.

### What you can't send

DMs accept text and photos only. You can't send voice messages, videos, files, or link previews.

## Managing your inbox

### Unread messages

When you have new messages, a badge appears on the Activity tab showing the number of unread conversations. Each unread conversation is highlighted in the list.

### Archive a conversation

To archive a conversation you don't need right now:

1. **Long press** the conversation in the list
2. Select **Archive**

The conversation moves to the **Archived** tab. To bring it back, open the Archived tab, long press the conversation, and select **Unarchive**.

### Delete a message

If you sent a message by mistake:

1. **Long press** the message
2. Select **Delete this message**

The other person sees an indicator that the message was removed in place of your original text. You can only delete your own messages.

## Notifications

You receive a push notification for every new message in a private conversation. The notification shows the sender's name, the message preview (or a "photo" indicator), and the product image if the conversation is about a specific item.

Tap the notification to jump straight to the conversation. If you're not receiving notifications, check that Jamble notifications are enabled in your phone settings.

## Important tips

- **Respond quickly.** Fast replies build buyer trust and can be the difference between a sale and a lost cart. Check your Activity tab regularly
- **Use the Sales tab** to focus on your sales conversations only
- **Send photos to answer questions** about condition, packaging, or shipping status. A photo is worth a thousand words
- **Don't share personal information.** Keep conversations inside Jamble. Don't share phone numbers, personal emails, or social handles in DMs
- **Block users who break the rules.** If someone is being inappropriate, block them from their profile. They will not be able to message you anymore

## Frequently asked questions

**Can buyers message me before they buy anything?**
Yes. Anyone can tap Message on your profile to start a conversation, even if they've never bought from you.

**Do I get a conversation for every sale automatically?**
Yes. Every completed transaction automatically creates a transaction conversation between you and the buyer, with the product and order details attached.

**Can I message all my past buyers at once?**
No. Jamble has no broadcast or bulk messaging feature. Every conversation is one-to-one.

**Is there a limit on how many messages I can send?**
There's a rate limit to prevent spam. Normal conversation speed is never affected. If the app flags that you're sending too fast, wait a few seconds and try again.

**Can I see when the buyer reads my message?**
Yes. You can see when your last message has been read by the other person.

**What's the difference between show chat and DMs?**
Show chat is public, everyone in the live show sees the messages. DMs are private, only you and the other person see the conversation. Use DMs for personal communication with specific buyers.

**Are DMs available on both iOS and Android?**
Yes. The feature works on both platforms with the same functionality.
