# Wishlist and Favorites

## What you'll learn

This guide explains where to find your wishlist in the app, how to save products and shows you care about, how to track your active offers after bookmarking, and how to set up notifications so you never miss the items that come back live.

## Before you start

You need:

- An active Jamble account (buyer or seller)
- The Jamble app installed on your phone

## Where to find your wishlist

Your wishlist sits on your profile page. It has two tabs:

- **Bookmarks** lists every product you saved (Favoritos in pt-BR)
- **Offers** lists the products where you have an active offer (Ofertas in pt-BR)

![Wishlist screen with Bookmarks and Offers tabs and product cards showing the heart icon](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/wishlist-and-favorites__wishlist-tabs__en__v3.png)

The **Bookmarks** tab is where your saved products go. Anything you saved while browsing or watching a show lands here.

The **Offers** tab is more dynamic. Every product that shows up here has an offer of yours in flight. Tap any card to see the current price and adjust your offer.

## Save a product

Whenever you browse the app or watch a live show, each product appears with its title, price, and photos. To save it:

1. Tap the **heart icon** in the corner of the product photo
2. The icon fills in red, and the product moves into your **Bookmarks** tab

![Product card with the heart icon filled in red in the top right corner, showing the saved state](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/wishlist-and-favorites__product-bookmark-cta__en__v3.png)

To remove a product, tap the heart icon again. It toggles off and the product leaves the Bookmarks tab. Saving a product does not identify you to the seller.

## When your wishlist is empty

If you open the wishlist for the first time, or if you cleared it out, Jamble shows an empty state inviting you to keep browsing.

![Empty wishlist screen with the heart icon and the message Start Exploring Deals](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/wishlist-and-favorites__wishlist-empty-state__en__v3.png)

The **"Start Exploring Deals"** prompt drops you straight into the product feed. Tap, browse, save one item, and it shows up in the **Bookmarks** tab right away.

## Save a show

Upcoming shows have a heart button in the preview. Tap to save.

Bookmarked shows appear under **Settings** > **Bookmarked shows**. When the seller goes live, you get a push notification (if the **Bookmarked shows** notification is enabled in your settings).

## Notifications

Jamble sends a push for every saved item, on the condition that the matching notification is enabled under **Settings** > **Notifications** > **Live Shows**.

- **Bookmarked shows** alerts you before a saved show starts
- **Bookmarked product from show** alerts you after a product from your Bookmarks is sold during a live show

Without those notifications, saving still works, but you only catch the event when you open the app.

## Important tips

- **Save before the show starts.** If you see a product you want in an upcoming show, save it right away. You will get the alert as soon as the seller goes live
- **Check the Offers tab regularly.** Every offer of yours in flight shows up there. It is the simplest way to track what you are competing for
- **Enable notifications.** Without them, Jamble cannot warn you in real time
- **Bookmarks are private.** Saving a product or a show does not identify you to the seller

## Frequently asked questions

**Where is my wishlist?**
On your profile page. It has the **Bookmarks** tab (saved products, Favoritos in pt-BR) and the **Offers** tab (active offers, Ofertas in pt-BR).

**What's the difference between Bookmarks and Offers?**
Bookmarks holds the products you want to revisit. Offers shows the products where you already placed an offer before the show started.

**Can I remove a saved item?**
Yes. Tap the heart icon again on the product or show. It leaves your wishlist.

**Does the seller know who saved their product?**
No. Bookmarks are private. Saving a product does not identify you to the seller.

**Is there a limit on saved products?**
No. Save as many as you want.

**Will I get a notification every time a saved product goes live?**
Yes, if the **Bookmarked product from show** notification is enabled. Whenever a seller starts selling a product you saved, you get a push.
