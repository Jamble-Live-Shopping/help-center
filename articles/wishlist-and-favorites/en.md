# Wishlist and Favorites

## What you'll learn

This guide explains how the wishlist and bookmark features work on Jamble. You'll learn how to save products and shows you're interested in, how to track your active offers, and how to use these features to never miss a deal.

## Before you start

- You need a Jamble account (buyer or seller)

## How it works

Jamble has two ways to save things you're interested in:

| Feature | What you save | Where it appears |
|---------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Bookmark products** | Individual products you want to buy or watch | Your wishlist, under **"Bookmarks"** |
| **Bookmark shows** | Upcoming shows you want to attend | **Settings** вҶ’ **"Bookmarked shows"** |

When you bookmark a product or show, you get notifications when something happens with it вҖ” like when a bookmarked product goes live during a show, or when a bookmarked show is about to start.

## Bookmarking a show

### Step 1: Find an upcoming show

Browse the home page or search for sellers. Upcoming shows appear with their scheduled date and time.

### Step 2: Bookmark the show

Tap the **bookmark icon** on the show card to save it.

### Step 3: View your bookmarked shows

Go to **Settings** вҶ’ **"Bookmarked shows"** to see all the shows you've saved. You'll receive a notification when a bookmarked show is about to start (if you have the **"Bookmarked shows"** notification enabled).

## Getting notified about bookmarked items

Jamble can send you push notifications for your bookmarked items. Go to **Settings** вҶ’ **Notifications** вҶ’ **Live Shows** to configure:

| Notification | What it does |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Bookmarked shows** | Notifies you before a bookmarked show starts |
| **Bookmarked product from show** | Notifies you when a product you've saved appears in a live show |

These notifications help you show up at the right time and not miss the products you care about.

## Why bookmarks matter for sellers

As a seller, bookmarks work in your favor:

- **Buyers who bookmark your products are interested.** They're more likely to show up for your show and place offers
- **Bookmarked products appear in the buyer's wishlist.** This keeps your products visible even after the buyer leaves your profile
- **Notifications bring buyers back.** When you go live and start selling a bookmarked product, the buyer gets a notification вҖ” even if they weren't planning to watch your show

**Tip for sellers:** If you notice a product getting many bookmarks, highlight it during your show. These are the items your audience wants most.

## Important tips

- **Bookmark before the show starts.** If you see a product you like in an upcoming show, bookmark it right away. You'll get a notification when the seller goes live with that product
- **Check your "Bids" tab regularly.** If you've placed pre-offers on products, the Bids tab shows all your active offers. This helps you track what you're competing for
- **Enable notifications for bookmarks.** Without notifications enabled, bookmarking still saves the item but you won't get the real-time alert when it goes live

## Common questions

**Where is my wishlist?**
Your wishlist is accessible from your profile page. It contains two tabs: Bookmarks (saved products) and Bids (your active pre-offers).

**What's the difference between bookmarks and the Bids tab?**
Bookmarks are products you've saved to view later. The Bids tab shows products where you've placed a pre-offer вҖ” meaning you've already committed an offer amount before the show starts.

**Can I remove a bookmark?**
Yes. Tap the bookmark icon again on the product or show to remove it from your saved items.

**Do sellers know I bookmarked their product?**
No. Bookmarks are private. Sellers cannot see which specific buyers bookmarked their products.

**How many products can I bookmark?**
There's no limit. Bookmark as many products as you want.

**Will I get a notification every time a bookmarked product goes live?**
Yes, if the **"Bookmarked product from show"** notification is enabled in your notification settings. Each time a seller starts a sale with a product you've bookmarked, you'll get a notification.
