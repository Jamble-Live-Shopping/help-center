# Show Management for Sellers

## What you'll learn

This guide covers the full lifecycle of a Jamble show, from the moment you schedule it to what happens after you end it. You'll learn how to manage your show before, during, and after going live.

## Before you start

You need:
- An approved seller account on Jamble
- A scheduled show with products added
- Your seller setup complete (payment info, shipping address, email)

## The show lifecycle

Every show goes through five stages: **Scheduled**, **Pre-show**, **Live**, **Ending**, and **Post-show**. This article covers what you can do at each one.

## Stage 1, show preview before going live

After scheduling your show, it appears on your profile. Tapping it opens the seller preview screen.

![Show preview with cover image, title, date and time, Bookmarks and Views counts, and Add listings button](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/show-management-for-sellers__show-preview-host__en__v2.png)

The **Bookmarks** and **Views** counters are admin-only stats, only you can see them. The product grid below **Add listings** shows the items already attached to the show.

From this screen you can:
- **Add products**, tap **Add listings** to create a **New Quickie Listing** or **Clone Past Shows Listings**
- **Reorder products**, hold and drag to change the order
- **Remove a product**, swipe sideways on an item in the grid
- **Edit or delete the show**, tap the menu button (three dots) in the top right corner

## Stage 2, edit or delete a scheduled show

When you tap the three-dot menu on the preview screen, an action sheet appears with **Edit** and **Delete**.

![Action sheet with Edit in purple, Delete in red, and Cancel in bold purple below](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/show-management-for-sellers__edit-show-menu__en__v2.png)

Tapping **Edit** opens the show edit flow. Tapping **Delete** brings up the confirmation **"Are you sure you want to delete this show?"** with **Delete** and **Cancel** buttons.

**What you can change before going live:**
- Show title
- Description and notes
- Tag (category)
- Cover image
- Date and time

**Postpone limit.** Jamble caps how many times you can postpone a show to a later date. When you hit the limit, you'll see a **Postpone Limit** alert with the message **"You just reached the postpone limit for this Show. It cannot be postponed anymore."**

**What you can change during a live show:** very little. Once live, only a few settings stay editable (like free shipping). Title, date, and tag are locked.

## Stage 3, entering the show room

When it's close to showtime, open your show and enter the host screen. You'll see a countdown and two action buttons.

![Host screen with Show starts in 12m 30s message, white Practice mode button, and red Go Live button](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/show-management-for-sellers__countdown-go-live__en__v2.png)

The countdown uses the text **"Show starts in"** followed by the time remaining (days, hours, minutes, seconds). The two buttons do the following:

- **Practice mode**, test your camera, audio, and product pinning privately. No one can see you
- **Go Live**, opens the **"Start your Show"** confirmation with the message **"Once started, people will be able to join you"** and the buttons **Yes, start** and **Later**

### What happens if you're late

The countdown message changes as time passes:

- **Up to your scheduled time**, "Show starts in" plus countdown
- **Up to 5 minutes after**, **"Time / to go live!"**
- **Between 5 and 10 minutes after**, **"Last chance to join or postpone the show!"** with a fresh countdown
- **More than 10 minutes after**, the countdown disappears. Your show is still on the calendar but you must start immediately. If you can't, open the menu and use **Late? Revive this Show** to pick a new date

**Don't be late.** Buyers who show up to an empty show won't come back. If you can't make it, use **Late? Revive this Show** in advance.

## Stage 4, managing the show live

Once you tap **Go Live** and confirm, your camera turns on and you're broadcasting. The host screen concentrates every control you need.

**Real-time sales dashboard (top-left).** The **Real-Time Sales** area shows your running total during the show, number of items sold, total revenue in $0.00, and average price per item.

**Audience counter (top-right).** A badge shows how many viewers are watching. Tap it to see the full list.

**Product list (bottom).** This is the core of hosting. The actions are:

- **Pin a product**, tap a product in the list to show it on screen. This is what buyers see and where they place offers
- **Start a sale**, with a product pinned, tap the action button. The behavior depends on the sell mode chosen at product creation (Real-time offers, Sudden Death, or Buy It Now)
- **Move to next product**, after a sale ends, the app suggests the next item from your list
- **Unpin a product**, tap the unpin button to take the item off screen
- **Run again**, if a product has remaining quantity after a sale, you can sell the next batch with one tap

### Live chat

Viewers send messages in real time. Greet people by name, answer questions, and keep a good pace between products.

### Camera flip

Tap the camera icon to switch between front (your face) and back (showing products up close). Many sellers switch back and forth throughout the show.

### Host settings menu

The menu button (three dots) opens an action sheet with all the key session actions.

![Host action sheet with End Show in red, How will I get paid, Audio Settings, Any question Bug or Feedback, Help Center in purple, and Cancel in bold below](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jamble-Live-Shopping/help-center/main/assets/mockups/show-management-for-sellers__host-menu-end-show__en__v2.png)

Here you'll find **End Show** (in red), **Audio Settings**, **How will I get paid?**, **Any question, Bug or Feedback?**, and **Help Center**. While in practice mode, this option becomes **Stop Practice**.

## Stage 5, ending your show

When you're done selling, open the menu (three dots) and tap **End Show**.

Before finishing, Jamble offers the option to **raid** another seller who's currently live, meaning send your viewers straight to their show. The in-app phrase is **"Before leaving, support the community by sending your viewers to a seller who's currently live!"** It's optional, but a great boost for the community.

After raiding (or skipping), tap **End Show** to finish the broadcast.

## Stage 6, post-show

Once ended, you'll see a summary of your show with stats:

- Total items sold
- Total revenue in $
- Number of viewers
- Show duration

From here, you can review your orders and start preparing shipments.

## Important tips

- **Add all your products before going live**, you can add during a show, but it's smoother with everything ready
- **Use Practice mode before your first few shows**, even 1 minute of practice helps you get comfortable with the interface
- **Don't go live late**, reschedule if you know you won't be able to attend
- **Keep your audience engaged**, talk to viewers, respond to chat, and keep a good pace between products
- **Pin products in order**, organize your list in the order you plan to show them. The app suggests the next one automatically
- **Watch your sales dashboard**, the **Real-Time Sales** counter at the top-left helps you track how the show is going

## Common questions

**Can I add products while I'm live?**
Yes, but it's better to add them beforehand. If you need to add something during the show, you can do it through the product management interface.

**What happens to unsold products after the show?**
They stay in your inventory. You can add them to your next show.

**Can I restart a show I ended?**
No. Once a show is ended, it cannot be restarted. You'd need to create a new one.

**How long can a show last?**
There is no strict maximum duration. Shows can run for as long as you want. However, very long shows tend to lose viewership over time.

**Can viewers join after the show has started?**
Yes, viewers can join at any point while the show is live.

**What if my internet drops during a show?**
If you lose connection briefly, the show doesn't end immediately. Try to reconnect quickly.

**Can I see who's watching my show?**
Yes, tap the viewer count badge at the top-right to see the full list.
