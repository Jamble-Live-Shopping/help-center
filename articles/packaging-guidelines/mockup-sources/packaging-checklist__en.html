Packaging checklist
What to use for each item type
Cards (Pokémon, TCG)
✓
Penny sleeve on each card
✓
Toploader over the sleeve
✓
Team bag or tape to seal the toploader
✓
Rigid mailer or small box with bubble wrap
Graded slabs (PSA, CGC, BGS)
✓
Bubble wrap around the slab (2 layers)
✓
Rigid box sized to the slab with padding
✓
Skip padded envelopes (they bend and scuff)
Hot Wheels and diecast
✓
Bubble wrap around the blister (2 layers)
✓
Cardboard box sized to the item
✓
Fill gaps with crumpled paper
