# Referral Program

## What you'll learn

This guide explains how the Jamble Referral Program works for sellers. You'll learn how to invite new buyers and new sellers, what rewards everyone gets, and how to share your referral links.

## Before you start

You need:
- An approved seller account on Jamble
- The Jamble app installed on your phone

## How the referral program works

As a seller on Jamble, you have **two referral options**:

1. **Invite buyers** — invite friends to buy on Jamble. You both get R$ 30
2. **Invite sellers** — invite other sellers to join Jamble. They get up to R$ 200, and you get R$ 500

Both options are available from your profile in the app.

## Step by step

### Step 1: Open the referral screen

Go to your profile and tap **Invite Friends** (to invite buyers) or **Refer Sellers** (to invite other sellers).

```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│  Profile                        │
│                                 │
│  ▶ Invite Friends               │
│    Earn R$ 30 per friend        │
│                                 │
│  ▶ Refer Sellers                │
│    Earn R$ 500 per seller       │
│                                 │
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Step 2: Share your referral link (Buyer referral)

When you tap **Invite Friends**, you'll see your personal referral code and a **Share** button. Tapping it opens your phone's share menu so you can send your link via WhatsApp, Instagram, or any other app.

Your friend receives a link like: `jamble.com/invite/buyer/yourusername`

**Here's how the buyer referral works:**

| Step | What happens |
|------|-------------|
| Your friend clicks your link | They land on a page where they enter their phone number |
| They download Jamble and redeem your code | R$ 30 is automatically added to their account |
| They buy 1 item during a Show within 14 days of redeeming the code | The referral is complete — you get your R$ 30 reward |

- Your friend gets their R$ 30 when they redeem your code
- You get your R$ 30 when your friend makes a purchase within **14 days** after redeeming the code
- Your R$ 30 reward expires **14 days** after it's credited — use it before it disappears
- Your friend's R$ 30 also expires after **14 days**

- Your friend has **30 days** to complete all steps
- Total reward for the new seller: **R$ 200** (R$ 50 + R$ 150)

- You can refer up to **200 sellers**

## How credits work

All referral rewards are **credits** applied to your Jamble account. They are used automatically at checkout to cover costs on your purchases.

**Credits have expiration dates**, so make sure to use them before they expire:
- Buyer referral rewards: 14 days for all
- Seller referral rewards: 14 days after being credited

## Important tips

- **Share via WhatsApp** — it's the most effective channel in Brazil. Your friends can tap the link and sign up in seconds
- **The more you refer, the more you earn.** Each buyer referral gives you R$ 30, and each seller referral gives you R$ 500. There's no limit on buyer referrals
- **Your referral code is unique to you.** It's based on your username and is permanent — you don't need to generate a new one each time
- **Credits expire** — remind your friends to use their credits quickly, and make sure to use yours too
- **The referral only counts if your friend is new to Jamble.** They must create a brand new account. Existing users cannot use referral codes

## Common questions

**Where do I find my referral code?**
Go to your profile in the app. Tap **Invite Friends** for your buyer referral code, or **Refer Sellers** for your seller referral code. You can also share your link directly — your friend doesn't need to type the code manually.

**My friend signed up but I didn't get my reward. Why?**
The reward is only credited after your friend completes the required action. For buyer referrals, they need to buy 1 item during a Show. For seller referrals, they need to complete their first Live. Check if they've completed this step.

**Do the credits work on any purchase?**
Yes. Credits are applied automatically at checkout when your friend (or you) makes a purchase.

**Can I refer someone who already has a Jamble account?**
No. Referral codes only work for brand new accounts. If someone already has an account, they cannot use a referral code.

**Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?**
For buyer referrals, there is no fixed limit. For seller referrals, you can refer up to 200 sellers.

**What happens if my friend doesn't complete the referral in time?**
If your friend doesn't buy an item (buyer referral) within 14 days, or doesn't complete their first Live (seller referral) within 30 days, the referral expires. You don't get the reward, and their initial credit also expires.

**Can buyers also refer people, or is it only for sellers?**
All users can use the **Invite Friends** feature to refer buyers. The **Refer Sellers** option is only available to approved sellers.
