# Flash Sales

## What you'll learn

A Flash Sale lets you sell a Buy It Now product at a discounted price for a limited time during your live show. You set a discount percentage and a countdown timer — buyers have to act fast before time runs out. This guide shows you how to set up and run Flash Sales step by step.

## Before you start

You need:
- An approved seller account on Jamble
- A show with at least one product listed as **Buy It Now**
- The Flash Sale toggle enabled on that product (you configure this when creating or editing the listing)

**Important:** Flash Sales only work with Buy It Now products. You cannot run a Flash Sale on a Real-time offers or Sudden Death product.

## How Flash Sales work

A Flash Sale is not a separate sell mode — it's a **feature you add to a Buy It Now product**. When you create or edit a product and set it as Buy It Now, you can toggle on the Flash Sale option and configure the discount and timer.

When you start selling that product during your show, the app asks you: **"Start as a Flash Sale or Buy It Now?"** You choose at that moment. This means you can have the Flash Sale configured but still decide to sell it at full price if you prefer.

## Step by step

### Step 1: Configure the Flash Sale on your product

When creating or editing a listing, select **Buy It Now** as the sell mode. You'll see a **"Flash sale"** toggle appear below the price field.

Turn it on to reveal two new fields:

![Flash sales configuration form showing price, discount percentage, and timer settings for Buy It Now mode](./assets/mockups/flash-sales__flash-sales-config__en.png)

- **Discount (%)** — The percentage off the original price. You can set any value from **10% to 90%**. If your product costs R$200 and you set 30%, buyers pay R$140

- **Timer (Seconds)** — How long the Flash Sale lasts, in seconds. Minimum **30 seconds**, maximum **600 seconds** (10 minutes). The default is 60 seconds

Save your listing. The Flash Sale is now configured and ready to be activated during your show.

### Step 2: Start the Flash Sale during your show

When you're live, find the product in your product list and tap **"Start"**. Because this product has Flash Sale enabled, the app shows you a choice:

![Flash Sales mode picker offering Flash Sale or Buy It Now options with descriptions](./assets/mockups/flash-sales__sale-mode-picker__en.png)

Tap **"Flash Sale"** to start the discounted sale. Tap **"Buy It Now"** if you want to sell at full price instead. Tap **"Later"** to cancel.

### Step 3: What happens during the Flash Sale

When you start the Flash Sale:

1. The product is **pinned** at the top of your show
2. A **countdown timer** starts (the duration you set)
3. Buyers see a green **"Flash Sale — X% OFF"** banner with the timer

![Flash sale card showing Nike Air Max 90 with 30% discount, price reduction from R$200 to R$140, 3 items left, 42 seconds remaining, and slide-to-buy button](./assets/mockups/flash-sales__flash-sale-card__en.png)

**What buyers see during a Flash Sale:**
- A bright green banner with "Flash Sale - X% OFF" and the discount percentage
- The original price crossed out, with the discounted price highlighted in green
- A countdown timer showing how much time is left
- A green "slide to buy" button (instead of the usual purple) at the discounted price
- The number of items left ("X left")

Buyers slide to buy. As long as the timer is running and there's stock left, anyone watching your show can purchase.

### Step 4: When the Flash Sale ends

The Flash Sale ends when:
- **The timer reaches zero** — the countdown expires
- **All items sell out** — every unit is purchased before the timer ends
- **You end it manually** — you can stop the sale early if needed

After the Flash Sale ends:
- If items remain, the product goes back to your product list. It can be started again as a regular Buy It Now at full price
- All purchases from the Flash Sale appear in your orders, just like regular sales

**Important:** While a Flash Sale is running, you cannot pin another product. The Flash Sale product stays pinned until it ends. Plan your show flow accordingly.

## Pricing example

Here's how the discount math works:

| Original Price | Discount | Buyer Pays | Your earnings (before commission) |
|---------------|----------|------------|----------------------------------|
| R$ 200 | 10% | R$ 180 | R$ 180 |
| R$ 200 | 30% | R$ 140 | R$ 140 |
| R$ 200 | 50% | R$ 100 | R$ 100 |
| R$ 200 | 90% | R$ 20 | R$ 20 |

Jamble's commission is calculated on the **discounted price**, not the original price. So if a buyer pays R$140, the 14% commission applies to R$140.

## Important tips

- **Build excitement first.** Before starting the Flash Sale, talk about the product. Show it up close, explain why it's special. Then say "Flash Sale starting now!" This gets more buyers ready to act
- **30% to 50% off works well.** A 10% discount doesn't feel urgent enough. A 30-50% discount creates real excitement. But make sure the final price still works for you — that's what you earn
- **Give buyers enough time.** 30 seconds is the minimum. For higher-priced items, use 60 seconds or more so buyers have time to decide and complete the purchase
- **Use Flash Sales to re-energize your show.** If the audience energy dips mid-show, a surprise Flash Sale brings attention back. Don't overuse them — one or two per show keeps them special
- **Announce it before starting.** Say "Flash Sale in 10 seconds!" so viewers are paying attention and ready to act when the timer starts
- **You decide at start time.** Even if Flash Sale is configured, you can always choose to start as regular Buy It Now instead. This gives you flexibility during the show

## Common questions

**Can I run a Flash Sale on a Real-time offers product?**
No. Flash Sales only work with Buy It Now products. If you want time-limited competition, use Real-time offers or Sudden Death instead — those already have a timer and competing buyers.

**Can I set a fixed amount discount (like R$20 off) instead of a percentage?**
No. Flash Sales use percentage discounts only. Set the percentage that gives you the final price you want.

**What happens if a buyer joins my show late?**
As long as the timer is running and there's stock left, anyone who joins can see and buy the Flash Sale product.

**Does the discount come out of my earnings?**
Yes. Your earnings are based on the discounted price. If the original price is R$200 and you offer 30% off, you earn based on R$140 (minus Jamble's commission).

**Can I stop a Flash Sale early?**
Yes. You can end it before the timer runs out.

**Can I pin another product while a Flash Sale is running?**
No. While a Flash Sale is active, the product stays pinned and you can't pin something else. Wait for the Flash Sale to end, then pin your next product.

**What if a buyer taps "buy" right as the timer reaches zero?**
There's a brief grace period. If a buyer initiated a purchase just before the timer ended, the payment can still process.

**How long can a Flash Sale last?**
Minimum 30 seconds, maximum 10 minutes (600 seconds). The default is 60 seconds.

**Can I change the discount percentage during a Flash Sale?**
No. The discount and timer are set when you create the listing. To change them, edit the listing before starting the sale.
