# Change or Fix Your Shipping Label

## What you'll learn

This guide explains what to do if you need to change or fix a shipping label on Jamble — whether the address is wrong, you need to regenerate it, or you want to add your own external tracking number.

## Before you start

You need:
- An approved seller account on Jamble
- A completed sale with a label already generated (or ready to generate)

## How labels work on Jamble

After a sale, your orders are grouped by buyer into **bundles**. Each bundle gets one shipping label. You generate the label from the post-show dashboard, and it's created as a PDF that you can view and print.

Once a label is generated, it includes:
- Sender address (your shipping address)
- Recipient address (the buyer's address)
- A tracking number
- A Correios barcode

## Step by step

### Fixing the shipping address before generating a label

If you notice your shipping address is wrong **before** generating a label, you can fix it directly from the dashboard.

**Step 1:** Open the post-show dashboard for your show

**Step 2:** Your shipping address is displayed at the top. If it's wrong, tap to edit it

**Step 3:** Update your address and save. The corrected address will be used when you generate labels

### Generating a new label

If you haven't generated a label yet for a bundle:

**Step 1:** Open the post-show dashboard and find the buyer's bundle

**Step 2:** Tap the button to generate the label. The system will create a Correios shipping label using the Melhor Envio service

**Step 3:** Once generated, the bundle shows an **open label** option. Tap it to view the label

### Viewing and printing a label

**Step 1:** From the bundle detail, tap the label action button

**Step 2:** The **Shipping Label** screen opens, showing your label as a PDF. If the shipment includes a content declaration, you'll see two tabs:

![Shipping Label screen with Label and Content Declaration tabs, PDF preview area, and Print button](./assets/mockups/change-or-fix-your-shipping-label__shipping-label-view__en.png)

**Step 3:** Tap **Print** to export the PDF. This opens your phone's share menu where you can print directly, save to Files, or send to another device

### Using an external tracking number

If you generated your own Correios label outside of Jamble (for example, directly on the Correios website or through another service), you can add the tracking number manually:

**Step 1:** From the order/bundle screen, look for the option to add external tracking

**Step 2:** Enter the tracking number or upload a photo of the label (the app can extract the tracking number from the image)

**Step 3:** The system will link the tracking number to the order, so both you and the buyer can follow the shipment

## Important tips

- **Always double-check your shipping address before generating labels.** Fixing it afterward requires contacting support
- **Generate labels for all bundles at once.** From the dashboard, you can generate labels for all buyers in a show with one tap — this is faster than doing them one by one
- **Print labels as soon as possible.** Don't wait too long after generating — ship promptly to keep buyers happy
- **Content declarations** are required for certain shipments and are generated automatically alongside your label
- **Keep the tracking number safe.** It's your proof of shipment if there's a dispute

## Common questions

**Can I regenerate a label after it's been created?**
Contact support if you need a label regenerated. Once a label is purchased through the system, it cannot be automatically regenerated from the app.

**What if the buyer's address is wrong on the label?**
If the buyer provided an incorrect address, contact support. They can help coordinate with the buyer to get the correct address and issue a new label if needed.

**Can I use my own Correios labels instead of Jamble's?**
Yes. You can generate your own label and add the external tracking number to the order. This is useful if you have a Correios business account or prefer a different service level.

**What is the Content Declaration tab?**
Some shipments require a content declaration — a document that describes what's inside the package. When required, it's generated automatically alongside your shipping label. Print both documents.

**I generated a label but now need to cancel the order. What happens?**
Contact support. If the label hasn't been used yet (the package hasn't been scanned by Correios), the label may be voided and a refund processed.
