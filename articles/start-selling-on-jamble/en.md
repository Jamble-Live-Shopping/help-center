# Start Selling on Jamble

## What you'll learn

This guide walks you through every step of becoming a seller on Jamble, from downloading the app to getting paid after your first show. Follow each step in order and you'll be ready to sell live.

## Before you start

You need:
- A smartphone (iPhone or Android) with the Jamble app installed
- A valid CPF (Brazilian tax ID)
- A bank account that accepts PIX
- Products you want to sell (fashion, sneakers, collectibles, lifestyle items)

## Step by step

### Step 1: Download the app and create your account

Download Jamble from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android). Open the app and create your account with your email or phone number.

Enter your Phone Number and confirm it via SMS or WhatsApp. Then fill your Email and add your CPF. Once it's done, your account is created and you need to apply for Live seller access.

### Step 2: Apply to become a seller

In the app, you'll see a screen titled **"Become a Live Seller"**. Tap the button that says **"Apply to Sell Live"**.

This opens a short application form. You'll be asked:

- **Your name and contact info**
- **What you sell** (clothes, sneakers, collectibles, etc.)
- **Your social media** (Instagram or other profiles where you sell, if any)

The form takes about 5 minutes. Our team reviews every application and may take up to 14 days to provide a final decision. You will be notified via in-app notification, email, and SMS once your application is approved.

### Step 3: Set up your profile

Once you applied, you can set up your profile so buyers know who you are.

Go to your profile and add:
- **A profile photo** - a clear photo of you or your brand logo
- **A username** - something buyers will remember (no special characters like ?,;/+&! and no "jamble")
- **A bio** - a short description of what you sell (e.g., "Sneakers originais e streetwear")

This is what buyers see when they visit your shop or join your show. A complete profile builds trust and brings more viewers.

### Step 4: Set up your payment info

To receive your earnings, you need to add your bank details. Go to **Profile > Settings > Seller Wallet** and click on **"Add bank details."**

Then, select the option that matches your status:
- **Pessoa Física (CPF)** for individuals
- **Pessoa Jurídica (CNPJ)** for businesses

You will need to add some personal information and your bank details (bank name, branch code, account number, and account type).

**Important:** Make sure all information is accurate. Once your registration type is selected, it cannot be changed later. Similarly, your CNPJ number cannot be updated — if needed, you will have to create a new account.

**This step is mandatory to be able to go Live for the first time.**

### Step 5: Set up shipping

Go to your seller settings to configure how you'll ship products. In Jamble, shipping works through Correios (Brazil's postal service).

What you can configure:

- **Label format** - choose the size of your shipping labels:
  - Half-page PDF (default, works with regular printers)
  - Full-page PDF
  - 4x6 thermal label (if you have a thermal printer)
- **Shipping preferences** - choose which shipping option you prefer to work with: SEDEX, PAC, Mini PAC

**How shipping costs work:** You don't set the shipping prices yourself. When a buyer purchases a product, Jamble automatically calculates the shipping cost based on the package size, weight, and the buyer's location. The buyer pays this cost at checkout. You just need to assign the right shipping profile (size category) to each product when you list it.

### Step 6: List your products

Before going live, add the products you want to sell. For each product, you'll enter:

- **Title** - be specific (e.g., "Pokémon Box 36 Booster Fogo Fantasmagórico Mega Charizard - Pokémon - Portuguese" not just "Pokémon")
- **Photos** - clear images showing the actual item
- **Description** - condition, size, brand, any details that matter
- **Sales Type** - understand the specificities of each sale type (real-time offers, Buy It Now, Flash Sale)
- **Price and Time** - your starting or fixed price for a limited time sale
- **Shipping profile** - the size that matches the package type (XS, S, M, L, XL, etc.)

**How to price your products:** If you plan to sell via real-time offers, you can set a low starting price (even R$1) to attract more buyers. The final price is decided by buyer competition. For Buy It Now (fixed price), set the price you want to receive.

Tips for your show setup:
- **Title** - tell buyers what to expect (e.g., "Camisetas vintage e streetwear")
- **Time** - evenings and weekends usually get more viewers
- **Show Cover** - a photo that clearly shows what you'll sell. This is the first thing buyers see
- **Show Recurrence** - specify when your show will repeat to maximise visibility and engagement

### Step 8: Go live and sell

When it's showtime, open your scheduled show and tap **"Start"**. Your camera turns on and you're live.

During your show, you can:
- **Start a real-time offer** where buyers compete by placing offers
- **Sell at a fixed price** using Buy It Now
- **Run a Flash Sale** with a temporary discount
- **Chat with your audience** in real time

You don't need to show your face. Many sellers point their camera at the products and talk about them.

### Step 9: Ship orders and get paid

After you end your show, you will see a dashboard grouping all your sales that you must **confirm within 5 days**. If not, they will be automatically cancelled.

You can retrieve this dashboard later: **Profile > Settings > Past Shows**

For each confirmed order:
1. **Generate a shipping label** - Jamble creates a Correios label automatically
2. **Pack the item carefully** and attach the label
3. **Drop off the package** at a Correios agency

Once the buyer receives the item, your earnings become available. Payments are processed via Pagar.me and will become available to withdraw depending on 2 factors:
- **The payment delay verification:** credit/debit card is 30 days, PIX is 1 day
- **Order completion:** the order must be delivered at the buyer's address and confirmed received in the app. The buyer has 3 days to confirm receipt — if they don't, the order is automatically marked as completed.

Once you initiate the payout by clicking on **"Withdraw"**, you will have to wait up to 3 days to get your bank's response:
- **Status completed** = bank accepted, you will receive the funds within 5 business days
- **Status failed** = bank rejected or error in bank details, check the error code on the payout

## Important tips

- **Set up a correct shipping address.** This is mandatory to be able to go Live.
- **Start with 5-10 products.** You don't need a huge inventory to begin. Even a few well-presented items can make a great first show.
- **Engage with your audience.** Greet viewers by name, answer questions, be yourself. Personal connection is what makes live selling work.
- **Ship quickly.** Fast shipping leads to good reviews, which brings more buyers to your future shows.

## Common questions

**How long does the seller application take?**
Our team reviews every application and may take up to 14 days to provide a final decision. You will be notified via in-app notification, email, and SMS once your application is approved.

**What if my application is rejected? Can I reapply?**
Yes, you can apply one more time (maximum 2 applications total). Review the feedback, improve your application, and try again.

**Do I need special equipment?**
No. Your smartphone is enough. Good lighting helps - sell near a window or use a desk lamp. A phone stand or tripod makes it easier to show products.

**How much does Jamble charge?**
Jamble takes a 14% commission on each sale. This covers the platform, payment processing, and buyer support.

**When do I get paid?**
For your balance to become available, the purchase must be marked as completed (delivered and confirmed) and the payment must have been processed. Payments are processed via Pagar.me — credit/debit card payments have a 30-day hold, PIX payments have a 1-day hold. You can then request a payout, which takes up to 3 days for bank processing.

**Can I sell anything?**
Jamble focuses on fashion, lifestyle, and collectibles. Items that are illegal, counterfeit, or dangerous are not allowed. Check the Terms, section 10 for acceptable use.

**Who pays for shipping?**
The buyer pays for shipping. The cost is calculated automatically based on the package size and the buyer's location.
