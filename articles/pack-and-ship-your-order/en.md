# Pack and Ship Your Order

## What you'll learn

This guide walks you through the complete shipping process after a sale — from confirming the order to getting paid. You'll learn how to confirm sales, generate labels, pack items, and drop them off at Correios.

## Before you start

You need:
- A completed sale (a buyer purchased one of your products during a show)
- Your shipping settings configured (address, label format)
- A printer to print your shipping label (regular or thermal)
- Packaging materials (box or envelope, tape, bubble wrap if needed)

## How the order flow works

After a buyer purchases your product during a show, the order goes through these stages:

| Stage | What happens | Your deadline |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| **Pending** | The sale just happened. You need to confirm it | 3 days |
| **Confirmed** | You confirmed the sale. Now you need to ship | 10 days |
| **Shipped** | You dropped off the package at Correios. Tracking begins | — |
| **Delivered** | The package arrived. Buyer has 3 days to confirm the contents | — |
| **Completed** | The buyer confirmed (or 3 days passed). Your payment is released | Up to 5 business days |

**Important:** If you don't confirm within 3 days or don't ship within 10 days, the sale is automatically canceled and the buyer is refunded.

## Step by step

### Step 1: Open your sales

Go to your profile and tap on your **sales** section. You'll see all your orders organized by status.

You can filter your orders using these tabs:

![Orders filter tabs showing All, Pending, Completed, and more options with list of orders below](./assets/mockups/pack-and-ship-your-order__orders-filter-tabs__en.png)

The available filters are:
- **All** — all your orders
- **Pending** — orders waiting for your action (confirm or ship)
- **Canceled** — orders that were canceled or timed out
- **Completed** — orders that are done
- **Under Investigation** — orders with an open dispute

### Step 2: Confirm the sale

When a new sale comes in, its status is **Pending**. Tap on the order to open it.

You'll see the buyer's information, the product they purchased, and two buttons:

![Sale confirmation dialog with buyer info and product details, offering Reject and Confirm buttons](./assets/mockups/pack-and-ship-your-order__sale-confirmation-dialog__en.png)

- Tap **Confirm** to accept the sale and commit to shipping the item
- Tap **Reject** if you can't fulfill the order (the buyer will be refunded)

**You have 3 days to confirm.** If you don't confirm in time, the sale is automatically canceled.

### Step 3: Generate the shipping label

After confirming, you need to generate a shipping label. The label is created through Jamble's shipping system — you don't need to go to a Correios website or create it manually.

From your order detail screen, tap the option to generate the label. The system will:

1. Calculate the shipping cost based on the product's shipping profile and the buyer's location
2. Generate a Correios shipping label in your preferred format (PDF or thermal)
3. Provide you with a tracking number

The label is sent to you and also available to download directly in the app.

### Step 4: Print and attach the label

Print the shipping label using your printer:

- **Half-page PDF** — prints on half a standard sheet. Cut it out and tape it to the package
- **Full-page PDF** — prints on a full sheet. Fold and tape it to the package
- **4×6 thermal label** — peel and stick directly on the package

Make sure the label is clearly visible and not covered by tape that could make the barcode unreadable.

### Step 5: Pack the item carefully

Before attaching the label, pack your item properly:

- Use a box or padded envelope appropriate for the item size
- Wrap fragile items in bubble wrap or packing paper
- Fill any empty space with paper or packing material so the item doesn't move
- Seal the package securely with packing tape

See the **Packaging Guidelines** article for detailed tips by product category.

### Step 6: Drop off at Correios

Take your labeled package to any Correios agency. Hand it to the attendant — they'll scan the label and give you a receipt.

**Keep the receipt** until the order is completed. It's your proof of shipment in case of any dispute.

### Step 7: Track the delivery

After dropping off, the tracking updates automatically. Both you and the buyer can follow the delivery progress. You'll see the tracking status in your order detail screen.

Common tracking statuses:
- **Pre-transit** — label created, package not yet scanned by Correios
- **In transit** — package is on its way
- **Delivered** — package arrived at the buyer's address
- **Returned** — package is being returned to you

### Step 8: Wait for buyer confirmation and get paid

Once delivered, the buyer has **3 days** to confirm the contents of the package. If they don't take any action within 3 days, the order is automatically completed.

After completion, your payment is released. Depending on your bank, it can take up to **5 business days** to appear in your account.

## Important tips

- **Confirm sales quickly.** The faster you confirm, the more time you have to ship. Buyers also appreciate quick responses
- **Ship within the 10-day window.** If you don't ship in time, the sale is canceled and your seller reputation is affected
- **Keep the Correios receipt.** It's your proof of shipment. If a buyer claims they didn't receive the package, this receipt protects you
- **Use the right shipping profile.** The shipping cost is based on the profile you assigned to the product. If the actual package is heavier or larger, you may need to adjust the weight before generating the label
- **Pack items securely.** Damaged items lead to disputes and returns. A few minutes of careful packing saves hours of support issues

## Common questions

**How long do I have to ship after confirming?**
You have 10 days to ship after confirming the sale. If you don't ship within this window, the sale is automatically canceled and the buyer is refunded.

**What happens if I reject a sale?**
The buyer is refunded immediately. Rejecting sales occasionally is fine, but doing it frequently can affect your seller reputation.

**Can I see the buyer's address before confirming?**
The buyer's shipping address is visible in the order details after the sale is created.

**What if I need more time to ship?**
The 10-day window is firm. If you know you can't ship in time, it's better to contact the buyer through the app to communicate the delay. For future shows, only list products you can ship promptly.

**How do I know when I've been paid?**
The order status changes to "Completed" once payment is released. You'll also see the earnings in your wallet.

**What if the package is returned to me?**
If Correios returns the package (wrong address, unclaimed, etc.), the order status changes to reflect this. You'll need to contact support to resolve the situation — either reship or refund.
