# Shipping Policy for Sellers

## What you'll learn

This guide covers the shipping rules and expectations for sellers on Jamble. Understanding these policies helps you avoid issues, keep your buyers happy, and maintain a strong seller reputation.

## The basics

When a buyer purchases a product during your show, you are responsible for:

1. **Generating a shipping label** from the post-show dashboard
2. **Packing the item properly** to prevent damage during transit
3. **Dropping off the package** at a Correios agency
4. **Providing tracking** so the buyer can follow their order

All shipments on Jamble go through **Correios** (Brazil's national postal service). Labels are generated through the Melhor Envio service integrated into the app.

## Shipping timeline

After a sale, you should ship as quickly as possible. Here are the expectations:

| Step | Deadline |
|------|----------|
| Confirm the sale | Within 3 days of the sale |
| Generate the shipping label | As soon as possible after confirming |
| Pack and drop off the package | Within **10 days** of confirming the sale |
| Buyer receives the item | Depends on Correios delivery time (varies by distance) |

**You have 10 days to ship after confirming a sale.** If you don't ship within this window, the order moves to "Ship Timeout" status and may be automatically cancelled and refunded. Ship as early as possible — don't wait until the deadline.

## What the buyer pays

The buyer pays for shipping at checkout. The cost is calculated automatically based on:

- The **shipping profile** you assigned to the product (determines package size/weight)
- Your **shipping address** (origin)
- The **buyer's address** (destination)
- Your **shipping speed setting** (Cheapest or Priority)
- Whether **package insurance** is enabled

You never pay shipping costs from your earnings. The buyer covers the full shipping cost.

## Shipping labels

### How to generate labels

After your show, go to the post-show dashboard. You'll see your sales organized by buyer in bundles. For each bundle, you can:

- **Generate a label** — creates a Correios label with tracking
- **Generate all labels at once** — processes labels for all buyers in the show

### What's included in a label

Each label contains:
- Sender and recipient addresses
- A Correios tracking number and barcode
- A content declaration (when required)

### Label format options

You can choose your preferred label format in **Settings > Shipping Preferences**:
- Half Page 8.5 x 11 (default)
- Full Page 8.5 x 11
- Thermal 4 x 6

## Packaging requirements

- **Use appropriate packaging.** The package must match the shipping profile you selected. A "Light Apparel" profile shouldn't be used for a heavy jacket
- **Protect the item.** Use bubble wrap, tissue paper, or other padding to prevent damage during transit
- **Seal the package securely.** Use tape to ensure the package won't open during handling
- **Attach the label clearly.** The barcode and addresses must be fully visible and not obstructed

## Tracking

Every label generated through Jamble includes a tracking number. Both you and the buyer can track the shipment.

- Tracking is updated automatically as the package moves through the Correios network
- The buyer receives notifications when the package status changes

If you use your own external label, make sure to add the tracking number to the order in the app so the buyer can track their package.

## Cancellations and non-shipment

### Seller-initiated cancellation

If you need to cancel an order (item lost, damaged, unable to ship), you can do so from the order screen. You'll be asked to select a reason:

- Unable to ship the item
- Item lost
- Item damaged
- Buyer requested cancellation
- Other reasons

**Warning:** Abusive or repeated cancellations may lead to temporary restrictions or account suspension.

### What happens if you don't ship

If you don't generate a label and ship the item within the expected timeframe:
- The buyer may request a cancellation
- The order may be automatically cancelled
- Your seller reputation may be negatively affected

## Nota Fiscal (Invoice)

For sellers registered as a business (with CNPJ), Jamble supports **Nota Fiscal** generation:

- **Automatic generation** — if your certificate is registered, the system can generate the invoice automatically
- **Manual addition** — you can add an invoice manually from the bundle detail
- **Exemption** — if you're not required to issue a Nota Fiscal, you can mark the bundle as exempt

## Important tips

- **Ship within the deadline.** This is the single most important rule. Late shipments hurt your reputation and frustrate buyers
- **Choose the correct shipping profile for each product.** An undersized profile means the actual shipping cost may exceed what the buyer paid, causing issues
- **Package items carefully.** Damaged items lead to returns, refunds, and bad reviews
- **Keep your shipping address up to date.** If you move, update your address in Settings immediately — it affects shipping cost calculations and label accuracy
- **Communicate with buyers if there's a delay.** If something comes up (weather, personal emergency), let the buyer know through the app chat. Transparency goes a long way

## Common questions

**Who pays for shipping?**
The buyer always pays for shipping. The cost is calculated automatically and added at checkout.

**Can I use a carrier other than Correios?**
Currently, all Jamble shipments are processed through Correios. If you need to use a different carrier, you can generate your own label externally and add the tracking number manually.

**What if a buyer complains about high shipping costs?**
Shipping costs are calculated based on the shipping profile, distance, and speed. Make sure you're using the correct shipping profile — an oversized profile will increase costs unnecessarily. If costs seem incorrect, contact support.

**How many days do I have to ship?**
You have **10 days** after confirming a sale to ship the item. If you don't ship within this window, the order may be automatically cancelled and the buyer refunded.

**What happens if I cancel too many orders?**
Repeated cancellations may result in temporary restrictions on your account. Only cancel when genuinely necessary (item damaged, lost, etc.).

**Do I need to issue a Nota Fiscal?**
If you sell as a business (with CNPJ) and meet certain criteria, you may need to issue a Nota Fiscal. The app will prompt you when needed. Individual sellers (CPF only) are generally exempt.
