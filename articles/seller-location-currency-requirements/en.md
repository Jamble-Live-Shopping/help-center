# Seller Location and Currency Requirements

## What you'll learn

This guide explains where you need to be located to sell on Jamble, what currency is used, and what documents you'll need to set up your seller account.

## Before you start

You need:
- A smartphone with the Jamble app installed
- A Brazilian phone number (+55)
- A valid CPF (Brazilian individual tax ID)

## Location and currency requirements

Jamble currently operates **exclusively in Brazil**. Country, currency, and document are detected automatically from your phone number.

![Requirements card showing Country Brazil, Currency Brazilian Real, Phone +55, and CPF required](./assets/mockups/seller-location-currency-requirements__location-requirements-info__en.png)

To sell on Jamble, you must:

- Be located in Brazil
- Have a Brazilian phone number (country code +55)
- Have a valid CPF

When you sign up, the app verifies your location and phone number. If you're outside Brazil, you won't be able to create a seller account.

**Buyers must also be in Brazil** to purchase from your shows. All your customers are Brazilian, keep this in mind when describing your products and communicating with your audience.

## Currency: R$ (Brazilian Real)

All prices, earnings, and payouts on Jamble are in **R$ (Brazilian Real)**.

- Product prices are set in R$
- Buyer payments are processed in R$
- Your earnings and payouts are in R$
- Shipping costs are calculated in R$

You don't need to select a currency, it's automatically set to R$ based on your location.

**Price formatting:** Jamble uses the Brazilian format. For example, one thousand reais and fifty centavos is displayed as **R$ 1.000,50** (dot for thousands, comma for decimals).

## Required documents

### CPF (required for everyone)

Your **CPF** (Cadastro de Pessoas Físicas) is your individual tax ID in Brazil. It's an 11-digit number, formatted as XXX.XXX.XXX-XX.

During seller account setup, you choose between **Individual** or **Company** and enter your CPF.

![CPF entry screen with Individual/Company toggle and input field for the tax document number](./assets/mockups/seller-location-currency-requirements__cpf-entry__en.png)

You'll need your CPF to:
- Create your account
- Place offers and buy products in shows
- Receive payouts for your sales
- Redeem discount codes

**Important:** Each CPF can only be linked to one Jamble account. If you delete your account, your CPF is locked and cannot be used to create a new account.

### CNPJ (optional, for businesses)

If you sell as a **Company**, you can optionally provide your **CNPJ** (Cadastro Nacional da Pessoa Jurídica). This is your 14-digit company tax ID, formatted as XX.XXX.XXX/XXXX-XX.

Having a CNPJ is not required to sell on Jamble, but it may be needed for certain business configurations.

**Important:** Once your CNPJ is registered, it cannot be changed. If needed, you will have to create a new account.

## Bank account requirements

To receive your earnings, you need a **Brazilian bank account**. When setting up your payment info, you'll provide:

- **Bank name** and bank code
- **Branch code** (agência)
- **Account number**
- **Account type**, checking (conta corrente) or savings (conta poupança)
- **Account holder type**, person (CPF) or business (CNPJ)

Your bank account must be in your name (matching your CPF or CNPJ). Payouts are processed via **PIX** (operated by Pagar.me) and usually arrive within minutes.

## Shipping address

You need a Brazilian shipping address to sell on Jamble. When setting up your address, you'll provide:

- **CEP** (postal code), 8 digits (e.g., 01310-100)
- **Street name**, auto-filled from your CEP
- **Street number**
- **Complemento** (apartment, suite), optional
- **Bairro** (neighborhood), auto-filled from your CEP
- **City and state**, auto-filled from your CEP

Jamble supports all 27 Brazilian states, from Acre (AC) to Tocantins (TO).

## Important tips

- **Have your CPF ready before you start.** You'll need it during the sign-up process, and you can't skip it
- **Your phone number must be Brazilian (+55).** The app uses WhatsApp to send verification codes, so make sure you have WhatsApp installed on the same number
- **One CPF = one account.** If someone else already registered with your CPF, contact support
- **Your bank account must match your identity.** If you registered with your CPF, your bank account must be in your name. Mismatched details will cause payout failures

## Common questions

**Can I sell on Jamble from outside Brazil?**
No. Jamble currently operates exclusively in Brazil. You need to be located in Brazil with a Brazilian phone number and CPF.

**What currency are my earnings paid in?**
All earnings are in R$ (Brazilian Real). Payouts are processed via PIX to your Brazilian bank account.

**Can I use a CNPJ instead of a CPF?**
You always need a CPF, even if you also have a CNPJ. The CNPJ is optional and only needed if you sell as a registered business.

**What if my CPF is already linked to another account?**
Each CPF can only be used once. If you see an error saying your CPF already exists, it means another account is using it. Contact support to resolve this.

**Can I change my bank account later?**
Yes. You can update your bank account details at any time in your payment settings.

**What happens if my bank details are wrong?**
Your payout will fail. You'll see an error message explaining the issue (e.g., "Invalid account number" or "Invalid CPF"). Fix your bank details and request the payout again.
